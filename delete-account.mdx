description: "How to permanently delete your Shapes account."
---

<Warning>
  Deleting your account is a one-time action and cannot be undone.
</Warning>

## How to Delete Your Account

1. Open the Shapes app.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Open the **Account** or **Profile** page.
4. If you are on a phone, tap the **Edit Profile** button.
5. Scroll to the bottom of the page.
6. Find the **Delete My Account** box.
7. Follow the instructions there to delete your account.
