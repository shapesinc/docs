<Warning>
  Deleting your account is permanent. You cannot undo it after the final countdown finishes.
</Warning>

## How to Delete Your Account

1. Open Shapes, Inc. If you are already in the app, stay there. If not, open [shapes.inc](https://shapes.inc) or [talk.shapes.inc](https://talk.shapes.inc) and sign in.
2. Open the sidebar menu and click **Account**.
3. You can also go directly to `talk.shapes.inc/u/your-username/account`.
4. Scroll to the bottom of the account page.
5. Find the **Delete account** card.
6. Click **Delete account**.
7. Read the warning, then click **Continue**.
8. Type your username when Shapes, Inc. asks you to confirm.
9. Click **Delete Account**.
10. A 30-second countdown starts. You can still click **Cancel** during the countdown.

If you do not want to delete your account, close the window or click **Cancel** before the countdown finishes.

## What Happens When You Delete Your Account

- This action is permanent once the final countdown finishes.
- Shapes and messages you created will not be deleted unless you delete them first.
- Chats you created will not be deleted unless you delete them first.
- Your account will be queued for permanent deletion and cannot be recovered.
- You will not be able to sign up again with the same email.
- The process may take up to 48 hours.
- Some data may be kept for legal reasons, as explained in the Privacy Policy.

## Before You Delete

Shapes, Inc. suggests a few things before you continue:

- Log out and take a break if you only need time away.
- Export your data first if you want to keep your memories.
- Contact support if something is broken or you need help.
