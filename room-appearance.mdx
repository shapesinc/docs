## Open the Room Appearance Menu

1. Click on your **Room title** at the top of the page.
2. Select **Room Appearance** from the menu.

## Upload Images

You can upload images to give your room a unique look:

- **Room Avatar** – Recommended size: **200 × 200 px**
- **Room Banner** – Recommended size: **1200 × 400 px**
- **Room Background** – High-quality wallpaper-style images work best

  <Tip>
    Keep in mind: this will appear differently on desktop and mobile views
  </Tip>

## Customize Background & Opacity

- **Background Opacity:** Adjust from **0–100%**
  - `0%` → Completely covers your background
  - `100%` → Fully transparent
- **Optional Gradient:** Add a gradient overlay to make text easier to read and create a polished look.

## Choose Text Bubble Colors

Pick colors that match your theme or extract colors directly from your uploaded background for a seamless design.

## Change Your Font

You can choose from any font available on [**Google Fonts**](https://fonts.google.com).

### How to Apply a Custom Font:

1. Visit [fonts.google.com](http://fonts.google.com) and select a font.
2. Open the **Embed** tab and copy the provided **CSS link**.
3. Paste the link into the font input field in your Room Appearance settings.
4. Enter the font family name (e.g., `Poppins`).

   Your new font will now apply to text inside this room only.

</Note>
