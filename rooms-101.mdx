## Click on your Chat Banner to locate your Chat Settings!

![Roombanner Pn](/images/roombanner.png)

<Info>
  Did you know you can regenerate, edit, add custom skills/flair on and reply messages? Check out the (:) menu next to a message!
</Info>

## Check out drop down guides below on this page for more info and click on the <u>links</u> that interest you for an in-depth guide!

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Invite humans" description="How do I invite my friends?" icon="mailbox-flag-up">
    - Copy a Chat invite link to send to your friends!

      ![Screenshot2025 09 19135620 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-09-19135620.png)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Add Shapes" description="How do I invite my Shape?" icon="circle-plus">
    - Search for new Shapes in the search bar
    - Invite Shapes you've created
    - Invite Shapes you've recently talked to from your friends list
    - Select shapes to invite from our lists of currently trending characters
    - Manually type in a specific Shape's vanity URL name

      ![Screenshot2025 09 19135741 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-09-19135741.png)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Manage Participants" description="How do I make Shapes respond to everything? How do I ban someone?" icon="thought-bubble">
    - Search for users in your Chat
    - DM, Kick or Ban humans
    - Dm, Activate or Deactivate Shapes
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Configure Shapes" description="How do I edit my Shape?" icon="comment-pen">
    - Select a Shape to edit
    - [Haven't created a Shape? Click here to learn how!](https://docs.shapes.inc/how-to-make-a-shape)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pinned Messages" description="How do I see messages I've pinned?" icon="map-pin">
    - View/Jump to/Unpin Messages from chat
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Search" description="How do I find previous messages or users?" icon="magnifying-glass-arrow-right">
    - Search for messages or chat participants
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Notifications" description="How do I edit what notifications I receive?" icon="alarm-exclamation">
    - Select to receive All/Mentions/Nothing as notifications for your Chat or simply mute them for a specific duration.
    - [Click here to learn more about notifications!](https://docs.shapes.inc/notifications)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="AI Configurations" description="How do I stop my Shape from sending extra messages or give them specific instructions?" icon="robot">
    - Disable/Enable [Free Will](https://docs.shapes.inc/multimessages#multiple-messages) messages from Shapes in your Chat
    - Write custom [Chat instructions](https://docs.shapes.inc/custominstructions-rooms) for all Shapes to remember when chatting in your Chat
    - Disable/Enable which Skills your Shapes are allowed to perform in your Chat
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Chat Appearance" description="How do I change how my Chat looks?" icon="card-heart">
    - Change your Chat name
    - Change Chat Avatar
    - Change Chat Banner
    - Change Chat Background
    - Change incoming/outgoing message colors
    - Provide a custom Chat font
    - [Click here for an in-depth guide!](https://docs.shapes.inc/room-appearance)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Moderation" description="How do I see who I've banned and block words?" icon="do-not-enter">
    - Enable/Disable allowing everyone to add Shapes to your Chat
    - Set a character limit for how long user messages can be
    - Create a slow mode to control how frequently people can send messages in your Chat
    - Block certain words from being used in your chat using the blocked words list
    - See a list of banned users
    - [Click here to learn more about moderation in your Chat!](https://docs.shapes.inc/create-a-room#moderation)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Chat Discovery" description="How do I set up my Chat for others to join it?" icon="asterisk">
    - Add a Chat description and tags to your Chat if you plan on sharing it in Chat discovery
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
