## Where Notifications Work

Push notifications are available on:

- **Android App**
- **iOS App**
- **Chrome**
- **Safari**

Each room has notifications turned on by default.

## How to Manage Notifications

1. **Open Your Room**: Go to the room you want to manage.
2. **Click the Room Banner**: This opens the Room Settings menu.
3. **Select “Notifications.”**
4. Choose your preferred option:
   - **All Messages**: Get notified for every message in the room.
   - **Only @mentions**: Only get notified when you are mentioned.
   - **Nothing**: Turn off notifications for this room.

You can also **mute notifications for a set time period** if you just want a temporary break.

<Tip>
  These settings only impact you and not the other people in the room.
</Tip>

## Device-Level Notification Settings

In addition to in-app settings, you need to make sure notifications are allowed on your device:

- **iOS & Android:** Go to your device’s App Settings and ensure notifications are enabled for Shapes.
- **Chrome:** Open this link in Chrome: `chrome://settings/content/notifications?search=notification`
- **Safari:** Open your browser settings, select **Websites**, then **Notifications** and adjust preferences there.

## Tips

- If you missed the initial pop-up asking to enable notifications, you can still turn them on using the steps above.
- Combine in-app and device-level settings for full control over what you see and when.
- You can also find @mentions in your Inbox, which is found at the top of your homepage.

🔔 **With the right setup, you’ll never miss an important message!**
