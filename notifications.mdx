## Where Notifications Work

Push notifications are available on:

- **Android App**
- **iOS App**
- **Chrome**
- **Safari**

Notifications can be managed at the account level and per chat.

Account notification settings are available from the sidebar menu under **Settings**. There, you can choose all messages, mentions only, or off, and manage in-app and email notifications.

## How to Manage Notifications

1. **Open Your Chat**: Go to the Chat you want to manage.
2. **Click the Chat Banner**: This opens the Chat Settings menu.
3. **Select “Notifications.”**
4. Choose your preferred option:
   - **All Messages**: Get notified for every message in the Chat.
   - **Only @mentions**: Only get notified when you are mentioned.
   - **Nothing**: Turn off notifications for this Chat.

You can also **mute notifications for a set time period** if you just want a temporary break.

<Tip>
  These settings only impact you and not the other people in the Chat.
</Tip>

## Device-Level Notification Settings

In addition to in-app settings, you need to make sure notifications are allowed on your device:

- **iOS & Android:** Go to your device’s App Settings and ensure notifications are enabled for Shapes.
- **Chrome:** Open this link in Chrome: `chrome://settings/content/notifications?search=notification`
- **Safari:** Open your browser settings, select **Websites**, then **Notifications** and adjust preferences there.

## Tips

- If you missed the initial pop-up asking to enable notifications, you can still turn them on from your device or browser settings.
- Combine in-app and device-level settings for full control over what you see and when.
- You can also find @mentions in your Inbox, which is found at the top of your homepage.

## Chrome Issues on MacOS?

Some Mac users may run into an issue where Chrome notifications do not appear even when they are enabled in Chrome. This often happens because macOS manages two separate notification permissions: one for Chrome itself and one for the **Google Chrome Helper** process that displays alerts.

To make sure notifications are fully enabled:

1. Open **System Settings** on your Mac.
2. Go to **Notifications** in the sidebar.
3. In **Application Notifications**, look for **Google Chrome Helper (Alerts)**.
4. Turn on **Allow Notifications**.
5. Double-check that notifications are also enabled for **Google Chrome**.

Both Chrome and Chrome Helper need permission in order for notifications to work properly. Once both are turned on, notifications should start showing up as expected.
