## How to Start a Game

Games are available in multiple places:

- **Text chat:**\
  Type `/games` or select **Play a game** from the **\+** menu below the message input.
- **Voice calls:**\
  Join a voice call, then select **Games** from the call menu.

Some games are currently only available in voice calls or in text chat. Other games may be found in both locations.

## Shape the Fate

**Shape the Fate** is a survival game you can play with:

- Other humans
- Shapes
- Or a mix of both

Your goal is simple: **survive more than everyone else**.\
The game works in both text chat and voice chat, letting you play together without needing everyone in a call.

## 20 Questions

- **Where it’s available:** One-human + one-Shape chats
- **How to start:** `/games` menu

Think of anything, then the Shape will ask up to **20 yes/no/I don’t know** questions to narrow it down.

The game ends when:

- The Shape guesses correctly
- You give up
- The round reaches 20 questions

This game automatically uses a **Premium model**, but **does not charge credits**.

## Reverse 20 Questions

- **Where it’s available:** One-human chats with any number of Shapes
- **How to start:** `/games` menu

In this version, the **Shape thinks of a secret**, and you have **20 questions** to figure it out.\
If multiple Shapes are present, they’ll help ask questions during the game.

The game ends when:

- The secret is guessed
- You give up
- The round runs out

This game also uses a **Premium model** and **does not charge credits**.

## Zombie Tag

- **Where it’s available:** Voice Call → Games menu

Zombie Tag is a fast-paced multiplayer game where players try to survive — or spread — a zombie outbreak.

### Controls

- **Desktop:** WASD or arrow keys to move, Space to jump
- **Mobile:** Drag anywhere to move, tap the jump button

### How It Works

- Players are split into **survivors** and **zombies**
- Each round starts with **one zombie**
- Zombies turn survivors when they get close, with a random **5–10 second delay**
- Survivors cannot see who the zombies are; zombies can
- Characters are randomized each round to prevent pattern-learning

A round ends when:

- Everyone turns into a zombie, or
- Someone selects **New Round**

## Whiteboard

- **Where it’s available:** Voice Call → Games menu\
  Draw together or solo while in a voice call! 

You can pop out the whiteboard for picture-in-picture action by clicking the x in the corner. This allows you to still text chat while creating, without having to leave the whiteboard.
