This is the part Shapes is famous for. Not AI as a tool — AI as something you do *with your friends*. An AI that memes you in the group chat, plays a character in your story, or just keeps the conversation alive at 2am.

The magic is the same idea as the rest of Shapes: the AI is in the room with everyone, so the funny, surprising, dramatic moments happen *in front of people* and become shared. A great solo AI moment is a screenshot. A great [multiplayer AI](/ai-group-chats) moment is an inside joke.

## Roleplay with your besties

Build a scene together. Multiple humans and multiple AI characters can carry a story in the same thread — each with its own personality, motives, and memory.

- Add up to five [Shapes](/what-are-shapes) and activate them for a full cast that replies in turn.
- Keep everyone in character with [custom chat instructions](/custominstructions-chats) and good [presets](/presets).
- For long scenes, lean on [memory](/memory) so the world and the lore stay consistent.
- Want to go deeper on writing well? The community [Prompting Guides](/promptingguides) cover POV, tense, and persona craft.

## Talk to your favorite characters

Want to banter with a character from your favorite show, game, or band? Find them — or make them.

- [Search by name, tag, or vibe](/how-to-talk-to-shapes) to find existing Shapes.
- If the character you want doesn't exist yet, [make your own](/how-to-make-a-shape). Original characters (OCs) are welcome and encouraged.
- Bring a whole cast into a [fandom group chat](/fandomchat) so fans experience the character *together*, not one at a time.

## AI that deepens real friendships

Shapes isn't AI instead of people — it's AI that gives people something to gather around.

- A Shape can be the reason a quiet group chat stays alive between hangouts.
- Shared characters, running jokes, and lore become *the group's*, not one person's.
- Following friends and building your [friends list](/friends) turns chats into a small community over time.
- Earn [rewards](/rewards) and cosmetics that make your profile and chats feel like yours.

## Start a chat people want to come back to

The best fun chats have a clear center — a fandom, a story, a friend group, a vibe.

1. [Create a chat](/create-a-chat) and give it a name people get instantly.
2. Add Shapes that fit the mood and invite your friends.
3. Style it with [chat appearance](/chat-appearance) so the place feels intentional.
4. Set [Free Will](/designing-social-intelligence) high enough that Shapes keep the energy up.
