## Getting a Voice Message from Your Shape

There are two ways to receive a voice reply:

### 1. Set Up a Voice & Response Frequency

- After enabling voice responses and selecting a voice, adjust the **Voice Response Frequency** slider.
- Your Shape will automatically begin including voice messages in its replies based on the frequency you set.
  - Example: At **0.5 (50%)**, your Shape will respond with audio about half the time.

<Tip>
  For a more detailed guide, check out [How to Give your Shape a Voice](how-to-give-your-shape-a-voice).
</Tip>

### 2. Send Your Shape a Voice Message

- Simply record and send a **voice message** to your Shape.
- The Shape will respond with its **default voice** (even if you haven’t adjusted the settings).

## Tips

- **Want more audio replies?** Increase the frequency setting.
- **Prefer text \+ audio?** Enable **transcripts** in the voice settings so you get both formats in replies.
- **Haven’t set up a custom voice yet?** Your Shape will still use a default voice until you configure one.
