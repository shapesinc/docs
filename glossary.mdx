The vocabulary of Shapes, defined plainly. New here? Start with [Core Concepts](/core-concepts) for how it all fits together.

**Activation** — Turning on a Shape so it responds to every message in a chat, no @mention needed. You can activate up to five Shapes per chat. Use the Participants menu or `/activate`. See [Chats 101](/shapes101#chats).

**Alive mode** — A setting that lets a Shape keep a chat warm by speaking up on its own during quiet stretches.

**Branch off** — Forking a conversation from any message to explore a different direction without losing the original. See [Branch Off](/branch-off).

**Chat** — A space where humans and Shapes talk together. Can hold people, Shapes, or both. Sometimes called a room. See [AI Group Chats](/ai-group-chats).

**Creator Dashboard** — Where you configure a Shape: personality, knowledge, memory, engine, voice, and more. At [shapes.inc/dashboard](https://shapes.inc/dashboard). See the [Shape Settings Reference](/shape-settings).

**DM** — A persistent one-on-one conversation with a Shape, distinct from a group chat.

**Engine (model)** — The AI model a Shape runs on — Claude, GPT, Gemini, and 300+ others. The raw intelligence under the character. See [Which Model Should I Use?](/choosing-a-model).

**Fallback engine** — The backup model a Shape uses if its primary engine is momentarily unavailable.

**Fandom chat** — A shared space built around a fandom, with characters and community context. See [Fandom Chats](/fandomchat).

**Fast forward (skip turn)** — Getting Shape replies without typing a new message — handy for moving a scene along. See [Shapes 101](/shapes101).

**Free engine** — An engine that costs no [Shape Credits](/credits). There's always a strong free option available.

**Free Will** — The per-chat settings that decide when a Shape speaks on its own: reply when mentioned, keep the convo going, react to keywords, always have something to say, and come back later. The heart of [social intelligence](/designing-social-intelligence).

**Group chat** — A chat with multiple participants — humans and Shapes — sharing one conversation. The core of multiplayer AI.

**Initial message** — What a Shape says when a conversation first starts. See [Initial Messages](/initialmessages).

**Knowledge** — A bank of facts you give a Shape (lore, custom command responses, relationships), recalled by relevance during chat. Don't overstuff it. See [Designing Great Shapes](/designing-shapes).

**Long-term memory (LTM)** — Durable memories a Shape keeps about people and events, recalled across conversations. See the [Memory Guide](/memory).

**MCP (Model Context Protocol)** — The standard that lets you connect custom tools to a Shape via providers like Smithery. See [Shape Skills](/shapeskills).

**Multiplayer AI** — AI that lives inside a shared conversation with other people, instead of alone in a private tab. The whole idea behind Shapes. See [What Is Multiplayer AI?](/articles/what-is-multiplayer-ai).

**Persona** — How a Shape sees a specific person — the name to call them and what to remember about them. Lets a Shape treat different people differently.

**Premium engine** — A frontier model that uses [Shape Credits](/credits). You can buy credits or earn them free through [rewards](/rewards).

**Preset** — The instruction layer that controls *how* a Shape writes (length, format, voice), separate from who it is. Uses the `{shape}` and `{user}` variables. See [Presets](/presets).

**Regenerate** — Asking a Shape to produce a new version of a message. See [Regeneration](/regeneration).

**Shape** — An AI character you can talk to or build, with a personality, memory, voice, and a role in the conversation. See [What Are Shapes?](/what-are-shapes).

**Shape Credits** — The currency used for premium engines and some premium features. Buy them or earn them free. See the [Credits Guide](/credits-guide).

**Shape Skills** — Tools that let a Shape *do* things beyond talking: web search, code, image generation, custom integrations, and more. See [Shape Skills](/shapeskills).

**Short-term memory (STM)** — The recent messages in the current chat that a Shape reasons over.

**Social intelligence** — The craft of a Shape knowing when to speak and when to stay quiet in a group. See [Designing AI With Social Intelligence](/designing-social-intelligence).

**Sponsored chat** — A chat where premium engine usage is covered, so everyone can use premium for free. See the [Credits Guide](/credits-guide).

**Time awareness** — A setting that lets a Shape know when messages were sent and how much time has passed.

**Training** — Example input/output pairs that guide a Shape's response style. It learns the pattern rather than repeating examples verbatim.

**Vanity URL** — A Shape's custom username/link. See [Vanity URL](/vanityurl).

**Vision** — A model's ability to understand images people send. Look for the **Native Vision** badge on the [engines page](https://shapes.inc/engines).

**Voice call** — A live, real-time spoken conversation with a Shape. Experimental and free. See [Voice Calls](/voicecalls).

**Voice message** — An audio reply from a Shape in its configured voice, optionally with a transcript. See [Give Your Shape a Voice](/how-to-give-your-shape-a-voice).

**`/wack`, `/reset`, `/sleep`** — Memory commands: `/wack` clears short-term memory, `/reset` erases long-term memory, `/sleep` saves the conversation to long-term memory. See the [Commands Reference](/commands).
