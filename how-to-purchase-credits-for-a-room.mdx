## Purchase Steps

1. Log in at [shapes.inc](http://shapes.inc)
2. Click the 👑 **Premium** button in the top-right corner of the screen
3. Select **Buy Shape Credits**
4. Select or enter the amount of Shape Credits you want to purchase
5. On the Confirm Purchase page, select **Sponsor a Chat**

   ![Select Sponsor Room Pn](/images/SelectSponsorRoom.png)
6. Choose the room you want to sponsor

   ![Select Which Room To Sponsor Pn](/images/SelectWhichRoomToSponsor.png)
7. Complete the transaction via **Stripe**
8. The selected chat will now receive the purchased Shape Credits!

---

## How Chat Credits Work

- **Shared Balance:** Credits sponsored to a chat are shared across all users in that chat.
- **Shapes in Chat:** Every Shape inside the sponsored chat will automatically use a premium model.
- **Default Model:** If a Shape doesn’t have a premium model selected, it will default to **Claude Sonnet 4**.
- **Checking Balance:**
  1. Log in at [shapes.inc](http://shapes.inc)
  2. Click the 👑 **Premium** button in the top-right corner of the screen
  3. Select **View Chat Credits**
