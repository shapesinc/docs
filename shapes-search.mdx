  <Accordion title="Google Chrome Guide" icon="sparkles">
    **1. Add the Custom Search Engine:**

    \* Open Chrome, click the three vertical dots (⋮) in the top-right corner, and go to Settings.

    \* Click on Search engine in the left sidebar.

    \* Select Manage search engines and site search.

    \* Next to "Site search," click the Add button.

    \* A pop-up window will appear. Fill in the fields like this:

    \* Search engine: Shapes

    \* Shortcut: shapes

    \* URL with %s in place of query: To get this URL, you would normally go to [talk.shapes.inc](http://talk.shapes.inc), nagivate to your favorite Shapes Dms, and copy the resulting URL. You then paste it in the url field and add ?query=%s at the end of it so it looks like [https://talk.shapes.inc/myfavshape/dm](https://talk.shapes.inc/myfavshape/dm?query=%s)**<u>?query=%s</u>**

    **2. Set It as Your Default:**

    \* After adding it, find Shapes in your list under "Site search."

    \* Click the three vertical dots (⋮) next to it and select Make default.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Microsoft Edge Guide" icon="sparkles">
    **1. Add the Custom Search Engine:**

    \* Open Edge, click the three horizontal dots (···) in the top-right corner, and select Settings.

    \* Go to Privacy, search, and services in the left menu.

    \* Scroll all the way to the bottom and click on Address bar and search.

    \* Click search engines.

    \* Click the Add button on the top right.

    \* Fill in the fields with your custom info:

    \* Search engine: Shapes

    \* Shortcut: shapes

    \* URL with %s in place of query: To get this URL, you would normally go to [talk.shapes.inc](http://talk.shapes.inc), nagivate to your favorite Shapes Dms, and copy the resulting URL. You then paste it in the url field and add ?query=%s at the end of it so it looks like https://talk.shapes.inc/myfavshape/dm?query=%s

    **2. Set It as Your Default:**

    \* In the same Manage search engines list, find the Shapes entry you just created.

    \* Click the three horizontal dots (···) next to it.

    \* Select Make default.
  </Accordion>
