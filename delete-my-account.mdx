### How to Request Deletion

To delete your account:

1. Send an email to [**hi@shapes.inc**](mailto:hi@shapes.inc) from the same email address linked to your Shapes account.
2. Include a short note that you’d like your account deleted. We'd love to know why, too, if you're comfortable sharing that information.

### Important Notes

- Account deletions are processed **manually** by the Shapes team.
- This is **not an immediate process**, so please allow some time for completion.
- Once deleted, your account and its data cannot be recovered.
- If you want to delete any **Shapes you’ve created**, you must do this manually from the **Settings** page of each Shape before requesting account deletion. Shapes are **not automatically deleted** with your account.
- After your account is deleted, you will no longer have permissions to manage, edit, or delete any Shapes created with that account.
