# Shape Premium?

If you're loving all the shapes you're using, Shapes offers a premium subscription that has the following benefits:

* Monthly allowance of Shape Credits to message your favorite shape
* Access to premium engines such as Sonnet 3.7, 3.5, DeepSeek R1, V3, OpenAI O3-mini, and more!
* Access to premium engine presets from its creator
* Access to upgraded image generation engines
* Voice messaging if enabled by its creator
* Web browsing through your shape

There are 2 available plans you can currently subscribe to.
