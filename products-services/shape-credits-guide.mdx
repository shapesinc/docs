# Shape Credits Guide

Keeping track of your credit balance is essential for all Premium Shape users. Your credits are consumed at different rates depending on several factors.

The most significant factor is your chosen premium engine (or the one selected by your shape creator). While we don't share the exact conversion formula, the guide below helps you understand how different models affect message efficiency and credit usage.

<Warning>
When multiple credit sources are available for a message, they're consumed in a round-robin fashion. This means for each premium engine message, the system randomly selects from your available non-zero credit sources. In practice, sometimes your personal credits will be used and other times shape engine credits will be used.
</Warning>
