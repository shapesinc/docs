# How To Purchase Gift Cards

**< Press the arrow keys to view the next steps! >**&#x20;

* First log in at [shapes.inc](https://shapes.inc/explore)
* Click the 👑 Premium Button
* Select Purchase Gift Card
* Select/Enter Gift Card Purchase Amount
* Complete Transaction Via **Stripe**
* Go to Gift Cards in order to copy your Gift Card codes to send to your friends via private message, email or text!&#x20;

### Additional Gift Card Info

<Steps>
  <Step title="Payment Method">
    Gift card purchases are made using your preferred payment method via **Stripe**. If you don't have a Stripe secure account, it's easy to make one during checkout!
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Subscription Gifting">
    If you're hoping to gift a subscription, then the minimum amount to gift would be $4.99 and the recipient will need to add their own Stripe account to pay the next month's subscription fee.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Payment Splitting">
    Subscription purchases **cannot be split between gift card account balance and additional Stripe payment** so please gift your friend the full amount needed for their subscription or simply allow them to spend the gift card on engine credits!
  </Step>
</Steps>
