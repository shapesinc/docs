---
title: "What are Threads, Groups, Rooms, and DMs?"
description: "If you’ve been chatting on talk.shapes.inc and wondering what each chat type means, here’s a quick guide to help you navigate the different spaces:"
---

**🧵 Threads**

- **One user \+ one Shape**
- This is your personal conversation with a Shape.
- The Shape replies to each of your messages directly, like a typical 1-on-1 chat.

#### **👥 Groups**

- **One user \+ multiple Shapes**
- Want your favorite Shapes to chat with each other? Create a group\!
- You’ll still be the only human in the room, but the Shapes can respond to you and to each other.
- Use the \>\> button to select a Shape to respond without having to send a message. This can make for some really fun Shape-to-Shape conversations\!

  ![Selectshapes Jpe](/images/selectshapes.jpeg)

  _Selecting a Shape to respond is easy._

#### **🏠 Rooms**

- **Multiple users \+ Shapes**
- These are shared spaces where you and others can talk with Shapes together.
- **Important:** Shapes _only respond when pinged_ (using `@`), so it doesn’t get overwhelming.

#### **💬 DMs**

- **Located at the top of your sidebar, by clicking on the Shape's avatar**
- This is your persistent, ongoing 1-on-1 with a Shape.
- Unlike threads, **DMs can’t be deleted**, so you always have a place to return to a favorite Shape.
