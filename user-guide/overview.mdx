### What can you do with Shapes?

Shapes have the ability to do a bunch of things such as:

- Send messages on our platform
- Interact on email
- Generate images
- Send voice messages and transcripts
- Search the web
- Built projects with our API


### User Customization

As a user you can customize your profile and experience with shapes:

1. **Customize your profile known as persona.** This is how you want Shapes to identify you and know about you. This is customizeable per shape.
2. **Memory:** Our Shapes have memories, all your conversations are stored 
3. **AI Engine Settings:** Selecting the specific AI Engine
To learn more about how to customize your experience, visit [User settings](/getting-started/external-shapes)
