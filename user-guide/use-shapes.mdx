# How to Use Shapes

Chatting with Shapes is designed to be natural and intuitive, but there are some tips and features that can help you get the most out of your interactions.

## Getting Started with Your First Shape

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a Shape">
    Browse the [Shape directory](https://shapes.inc/explore) and select a Shape that interests you
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read Their Profile">
    Check their description, personality, and capabilities before starting
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the Conversation">
    Click "Start Chat" or "Message" to begin your interaction
  </Step>
  <Step title="Introduce Yourself">
    Many Shapes appreciate a brief introduction about who you are and what you're looking for
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Basic Chat Features

### Sending Messages

Simply type your message in the chat box and press Enter or click Send. Shapes can handle:

- **Text Messages**: Regular conversation and questions
- **Long Messages**: Detailed requests or complex topics
- **Follow-up Questions**: Building on previous conversations
- **Context Switching**: Moving between different topics

## Advanced Features

### Voice Messages

Many Shapes support voice interactions:

- **Voice Input**: Speak your message instead of typing
- **Voice Responses**: Hear Shapes respond in their unique voice
- **Transcripts**: Get text versions of voice conversations
- **Voice Settings**: Adjust speed, tone, and language preferences

### Image Generation

Premium Shapes can create images:

```
"Can you create an image of a sunset over mountains?"
"Generate a logo for my coffee shop"
"Draw a cartoon character based on this description"
```

### Web Search

Premium Shapes can search the web for current information:

```
"What's the latest news about space exploration?"
"Find me recent reviews of the iPhone 15"
"Search for healthy dinner recipes"
```

### Email Integration

Some Shapes can send emails:

- **Reminders**: Set up email reminders for important tasks
- **Summaries**: Get conversation summaries sent to your email
- **Notifications**: Receive updates about Shape activities

## Communication Tips

### Getting Better Responses

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Be Specific">
    **Instead of**: "Help me with writing"
    **Try**: "Help me write a professional email to my boss about taking time off"
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Provide Context">
    **Instead of**: "What should I do?"
    **Try**: "I'm a college student struggling with time management. What study techniques would work best for someone with ADHD?"
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Ask Follow-ups">
    **Instead of**: Starting over with new topics
    **Try**: "Can you expand on that second point?" or "What would be an example of that?"
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Set Expectations">
    **Instead of**: Assuming capabilities
    **Try**: "Can you help me with coding, or should I find a different Shape for that?"
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Conversation Etiquette

- **Be Patient**: Complex requests may take a moment to process
- **Be Clear**: If a response isn't what you expected, clarify your request
- **Be Respectful**: Shapes respond better to polite, respectful communication
- **Be Specific**: The more details you provide, the better the response

## Managing Your Conversations

### Conversation History

- **Access Past Chats**: All your conversations are saved and searchable
- **Continue Conversations**: Pick up where you left off, even days later
- **Search Messages**: Find specific topics or information from past chats
- **Export Conversations**: Download your chat history for your records

### Memory Management

Shapes remember your conversations, but you can control this:

- **Personal Memory**: What the Shape remembers about you
- **Conversation Memory**: How much chat history they retain
- **Shared Memory**: Information shared between different Shapes (if enabled)
- **Memory Reset**: Clear memories for a fresh start

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Shape Not Responding as Expected

<Accordion title="Shape seems confused or off-topic">
  Try rephrasing your question or providing more context. Some Shapes work better with specific types of requests.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Response quality is poor">
  Check if you're using the right AI engine for your needs. Premium engines often provide better responses for complex tasks.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Shape doesn't remember previous conversation">
  Check your memory settings in the dashboard. You may need to adjust how much conversation history the Shape retains.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Features not working">
  Ensure you have the right subscription level for premium features like image generation or web search.
</Accordion>

### Technical Issues

<Accordion title="Messages not sending">
  Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, contact support.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Slow responses">
  High traffic times may cause delays. Premium users typically get faster response times.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Voice features not working">
  Check your browser permissions for microphone access and ensure you're using a supported browser.
</Accordion>

## Best Practices

### For Learning and Education

- **Ask for explanations**: "Can you explain this concept in simple terms?"
- **Request examples**: "Can you give me some examples of this?"
- **Test understanding**: "Can you quiz me on what we just discussed?"
- **Break down complex topics**: "Let's tackle this step by step"

### For Creative Work

- **Brainstorm together**: "Let's come up with ideas for..."
- **Iterate on ideas**: "That's good, but can we make it more..."
- **Get feedback**: "What do you think about this approach?"
- **Explore alternatives**: "What are some other ways to..."

### For Professional Tasks

- **Be specific about requirements**: Include deadlines, format, audience
- **Ask for structure**: "Can you outline this before we dive in?"
- **Request revisions**: "Can you make this more formal/casual/detailed?"
- **Verify information**: "Can you double-check these facts?"
