Shapes.inc dashboard gives you powerful controls to customize how you interact with each Shape. From memory settings to AI engine preferences, you can tailor every aspect of your experience.

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is your control center for managing your Shapes experience. Here you can:

- Manage your conversations and memories
- Adjust AI engine settings
- Customize your persona per Shape
- Monitor your usage and billing

## Memory Settings

Manage your conversation history and memories by visiting the memory tab.

## AI Engine Settings

Choose the AI engine that best fits your needs for each Shape:

### Engine Options

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Standard">
    **Best for**: General conversations, everyday interactions

    - Balanced performance and cost
    - Good for most use cases
    - Reliable and consistent responses
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Premium">
    **Best for**: Complex conversations, creative tasks

    - Enhanced reasoning capabilities
    - Better context understanding
    - More nuanced responses
    - Available with Premium subscription / Shape credits
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
