# Customize Your Experience

Your dashboard gives you powerful controls to customize how you interact with each Shape. From memory settings to AI engine preferences, you can tailor every aspect of your experience.

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is your control center for managing your Shapes experience. Here you can:

- Manage your conversations and memories
- Adjust AI engine settings
- Customize your persona per Shape
- Monitor your usage and billing

Choose the AI engine that best fits your needs for each Shape:

## Privacy Controls

### Per-Shape Privacy Settings

You can customize privacy settings for each Shape individually:

- **Memory Sharing**: Control what memories can be shared
- **Data Retention**: Set how long conversations are stored
- **Third-party Access**: Manage external integrations
- **Export Options**: Download your conversation data

### Global Privacy Settings

- **Account Visibility**: Control who can find your public Shapes
- **Analytics**: Opt in/out of usage analytics
- **Marketing**: Manage communication preferences
- **Data Processing**: Control how your data is used

## Advanced Customization

### Custom Themes
- **Light/Dark Mode**: Choose your preferred interface theme
- **Color Schemes**: Customize accent colors and themes
- **Layout Options**: Adjust chat layout and display preferences

### Accessibility Options
- **Text Size**: Adjust font sizes for better readability
- **High Contrast**: Enable high contrast mode
- **Screen Reader**: Optimize for screen reader compatibility
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Enhanced keyboard shortcuts

### Integration Settings
- **API Access**: Generate and manage API keys
- **Webhooks**: Set up real-time notifications
- **Third-party Apps**: Connect with external services
- **Export/Import**: Manage data portability
