# How to Find Shapes

Finding the right Shape for your needs is easy with our powerful search tools and intelligent recommendation system.

## Search for Shapes

### Basic Search

Use the search bar at the top of any page to find Shapes by:

- **Name**: Search for specific Shape names
- **Keywords/tags**: Find Shapes by their adjectives, identifiers, or purpose
- **Description**: Match words from Shape descriptions
- **vibe search**: Search for Shapes by their vibe

## Suggested Shapes in Profiles

When viewing any Shape's profile, you'll find personalized suggestions to help you discover similar or complementary characters.

### Types of Suggestions

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Similar Shapes">
    Shapes with comparable personalities, capabilities, or purposes to the one you're viewing
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="By Same Creator">
    Other Shapes created by the same person, often with consistent quality and style
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Frequently Used Together">
    Shapes that other users often interact with alongside the current one
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="You Might Like">
    Personalized recommendations based on your interaction history and preferences
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### How Suggestions Work

Our recommendation system considers:

- **Your interaction history** with similar Shapes
- **Shape categories** and tags
- **Creator relationships** and collaboration networks

## Search Results

### Understanding Results

Search results show:

- **Shape name and avatar**
- **Brief description**
- **Interaction Buttons**

After finding the shape you're looking for, you can click the **Chat Now** button to start chatting with the shape.
