# Personalize Your Experience

Your persona is how Shapes identify and remember you across conversations. By customizing your profile, you can create more meaningful and personalized interactions with AI characters.

## Setting Up Your Persona

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access User Settings">
    Navigate to your dashboard and click on "User Settings" or "Profile"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Persona">
    Fill out information about yourself that you want Shapes to know:
    - Your name and preferred pronouns
    - Your interests and hobbies
    - Your communication style preferences
    - Any relevant background information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Per Shape">
    You can create different personas for different Shapes, allowing for varied interactions
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to Include in Your Persona

### Basic Information
- **Name**: How you'd like to be addressed
- **Age Range**: If relevant to your interactions
- **Location**: General location (city/country) if you're comfortable sharing
- **Pronouns**: Your preferred pronouns

### Interests & Hobbies
- **Favorite Activities**: What you enjoy doing in your free time
- **Professional Background**: Your work or study area (if relevant)
- **Entertainment Preferences**: Movies, books, games, music you enjoy
- **Learning Goals**: What you're trying to learn or improve

### Communication Style
- **Tone Preference**: Formal, casual, friendly, professional
- **Conversation Topics**: What you like to discuss
- **Boundaries**: Topics you prefer to avoid

## Privacy & Safety

<Warning>
  Only share information you're comfortable with AI characters knowing. Avoid sharing:
  - Personal identifying information (full address, phone numbers)
  - Financial information
  - Sensitive personal details
</Warning>

## Per-Shape Customization

You can create different personas for different types of Shapes:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Professional Shapes" icon="briefcase">
    Use a more formal persona for work-related or educational Shapes
  </Card>
  <Card title="Entertainment Shapes" icon="gamepad-2">
    Create a fun, casual persona for gaming or entertainment Shapes
  </Card>
  <Card title="Learning Shapes" icon="graduation-cap">
    Focus on your learning goals and current knowledge level
  </Card>
  <Card title="Creative Shapes" icon="palette">
    Highlight your creative interests and projects
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Managing Your Personas

- **Edit Anytime**: You can update your persona information at any time
- **Multiple Personas**: Create different personas for different contexts
- **Privacy Controls**: Choose what information to share with each Shape
- **Reset Option**: Start fresh with a new persona if needed

<Tip>
  The more thoughtful and detailed your persona, the better Shapes can tailor their responses to your interests and communication style.
</Tip>
