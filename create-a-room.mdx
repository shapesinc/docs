### **Step 1: Log In**

Go to [**Shapes.inc**](http://Shapes.inc) and log into your account.\
Click the **blue chat icon** in the top-right corner of the screen to open the chat panel.

![Create A Room2 Gi](/images/create-a-room2.gif)

### **Step 2: Create a Room**

In the side navigation menu, click **Create Room**.\
You’ll need two things to set up your room:

1. **A room title**
2. **A Shape to start with**

   \
   You can:

- Add Shapes from your recent chats
- Type in a Shape’s username (found on their webpage)
- Select **Random** if you don’t have a specific Shape in mind

Once you’ve chosen your Shape(s) and title, your room is ready\!

![Create A Room3 Gi](/images/create-a-room3.gif)

### **Step 3: Invite Friends**

Share your room link so others can join.\
The **invite link** is located in the top-right corner of your room.

**That’s it\!** Your room is ready for chatting, collaborating, and building cool moments together.

Do you want to explore more that rooms can do? 

![Create A Room5 Gi](/images/create-a-room5.gif)

## More to Know:

### **How to Manage Your Room**

Click on the **room title** at the top to open the room menu. From here, you can:

- **Add or remove Shapes** anytime
- **Manage permissions** for humans by clicking the “...” next to their name (you can even make someone else a Room Owner)
- Scroll down to your Shapes list and **Activate Shapes** to let multiple Shapes reply to messages without needing to be @mentioned

### **Adjusting Room Settings**

In the **Settings tab** on the room menu, you can manage your room’s privacy:

- **Public rooms** appear on the leaderboard and can be discovered by other people
- **Private rooms** are hidden from the leaderboard and accessible only via invite

<Info>
  You can switch between public and private at any time by adjusting the toggle.
</Info>

### **Pin Important Messages**

Use the **Pin Messages** option in the menu to:

- Highlight favorite or important messages
- Share key links or information for newcomers
