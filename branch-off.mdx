---
title: "Branch Off"
description: "Continue your favourite conversations in a new private space."
---

Ever had such a great conversation that you wished you could keep it going in a new direction?\
Well, now you can - just **Branch Off** any Shape’s (or user’s) message!

**Branch Off** creates a **new private chat** starting from the message you choose.\
It automatically copies up to the **last 50 messages**, ending with your selected one - so you can pick up right where the conversation left off.

### Why it’s cool

- Keep the main chat clean while diving deeper into a specific topic.
- Start private discussions without losing context.
- Perfect for testing Shapes, brainstorming, or focused one-on-one chats.

### How to use it

1. Click the **three-dot menu** next to any message.
2. Choose **“Branch Off.”**
3. A new chat will instantly be created - you’ll be redirected there.

Now go ahead - keep that conversation branching!
