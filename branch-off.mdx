---
title: "Branch Off"
description: "Continue your favourite conversations in a new private space."
---

Use **Branch Off** when a group chat goes in a direction you want to continue privately.

**Branch Off** creates a **new private chat** starting from the message you choose.\
It automatically copies up to the **last 50 messages**, ending with your selected one, so the new chat starts with the right context.

### Why it’s cool

- Keep the main chat clean while diving deeper into a specific topic.
- Start private discussions without losing the thread.
- Perfect for testing Shapes, brainstorming, or focused one-on-one chats.

### How to use it

1. Click the **three-dot menu** next to any message.
2. Choose **“Branch Off.”**
3. A new chat will instantly be created - you’ll be redirected there.

Branch Off is available in chats, but not in direct messages.
