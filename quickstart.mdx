The whole point of Shapes is AI you experience *with other people*. So this quickstart gets you to exactly that: a real conversation with a Shape, then a group chat with a friend in it. A few minutes, no setup.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up — it's free">
    Create an account at [shapes.inc](https://shapes.inc) or on the [iOS / Android app](https://shapes.inc). No card, no limits to start.

    {/* SCREENSHOT: the Shapes sign-up / login screen on web. */}
    ![Sign in to Shapes](/images/signin.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find a Shape that interests you">
    Search by name, browse by category, or just search a *vibe* ("grumpy chef", "study buddy", "dungeon master"). Every Shape is an AI character someone built — with a personality, memory, and a voice.

    New to browsing? See [How to find and talk to Shapes](/how-to-talk-to-shapes).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start talking">
    Open a Shape and just type. Try a voice note, send it an image, or ask it to `/imagine` something. This is your private space to get a feel for it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make it a group chat">
    Here's the part you can't do anywhere else. Open the sidebar, hit **New Chat**, and add a Shape *and* a friend. Now you're all in the same room — humans and AI together.

    Full walkthrough: [Create an AI group chat](/create-a-chat).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite a friend">
    Open the chat menu and **Invite Friends** — share the link anywhere. When they join, the Shape responds to all of you, reacts to the back-and-forth, and remembers what happens.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## A few things worth knowing on day one

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="It's multiplayer — add up to five Shapes" icon="users-round">
    A chat can hold humans, Shapes, or both. You can **activate up to five Shapes** in one room, and the activated ones reply to messages in turn. @mention a specific Shape to call on it directly. More in [AI Group Chats](/ai-group-chats).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="300+ AI models, most of them free" icon="cpu">
    Every Shape runs on a model — Claude, GPT, Gemini, Grok, DeepSeek, and many more. Most cost nothing. The frontier **premium** engines use [Shape Credits](/credits), which you can buy or earn free through [rewards](/rewards). A free engine is always available, so you never have to pay to keep chatting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Shapes can do more than talk" icon="wrench">
    They send voice messages, generate images, read PDFs, search the web, run code, and remember you. See the full list in [What Shapes Can Do](/capabilities).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Quick commands" icon="terminal">
    Type `/` in any chat to see commands — `/imagine` for an image, `/web` to search, `/activate` to make a Shape respond to everything. Full list in the [Commands Reference](/commands).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
