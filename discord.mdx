    Recover your Shapes account for users who used to use Discord sign in\
<Tip>You'll need to know the email address associated with your Discord Account that you used to use to sign into Shapes.inc with. If you don't know this information, please reach out to us at hi@shapes.inc </Tip>
    
    Head over to Shapes.inc. Select the Log in button found in the top right corner.
 
    If your Discord associated email address was Gmail, use Google sign in. This should automatically connect you into your account. \
    If your Discord associated email address was not Gmail, select email sign in. \
 \
    For email sign in, you will need to scroll to the "Sign up" button at the bottom. Once you sign up, you can authorize your email. After authorizing your email address, it should automatically connect you into your previously created Shapes account.

<Tip>
  Still having trouble signing in? Send us an email: hi@shapes.inc
</Tip>
