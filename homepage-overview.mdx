![Homepage2 Pn](/images/homepage2.png)

1. **Home Dashboard**: Click on the Shapes, Inc. logo anytime to return to your home dashboard.
2. **Subscribe to Premium Features**: Check out [Products & Services](https://wiki.shapes.inc/new-to-shapes-guide/products-and-services) to learn more in detial
3. **Account Management**: Click your profile icon to access "My Account," get "Help," or "Log Out."
4. **Search**: Use the search bar to explore and find new or specific Shapes.
5. **Create Shape**: Creating a Shape is completely FREE\! Head to "[How to make a shape](https://wiki.shapes.inc/new-to-shapes-guide/faqs/readme)" to get started.
6. **Continue**: Find Shapes you've previously chatted with.
7. **Made by You**: Access Shapes you've personally created.
