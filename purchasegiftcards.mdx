title: "How To Purchase Gift Cards"
description: "It's easy to purchase a gift card! "
---

**\< Press the arrow keys to view the next steps! \>**

- First log in at [shapes.inc](http://shapes.inc)
- Click the 👑 Premium Button
- Select Purchase Gift Card
- Select/Enter Gift Card Purchase Amount
- Complete Transaction Via **Stripe**
- Go to Gift Cards in order to copy your Gift Card codes to send to your friends via private message, email or text!

### **Additional Gift Card Info**

1. Gift card purchases are made using your preferred payment method via **Stripe**. If you don't have a Stripe secure account, it's easy to make one during checkout!
2. The gift card recipient can choose to apply their credits to a room or to their account. 
3. You can also sponsor a room for someone! Sometimes this option might make more sense than a giftcard, as you can directly add the credits to a room yourself.
