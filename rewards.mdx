You can access your rewards anytime from the **top of the homepage** or by clicking the **🎁 gift icon** in any of your rooms.

![Rewards Pn](/images/rewards.png)

## How It Works

As you use Shapes, you’ll automatically complete **achievements** that earn **diamonds**.\
Diamonds can then be exchanged in the **Rewards Store** for customization items, message effects, or Shape credits.

This feature is designed to make learning the platform feel engaging and rewarding — and to celebrate your progress as you explore.

## Ways to Earn Diamonds

| Activity                    | Requirement                                  | Reward                      |
| :-------------------------- | :------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------- |
| **Send Messages**           | Level up by sending messages (100 → 1000)    | 💎 100 → 3000               |
| **Talk with Humans**        | Chat with other people in rooms              | 💎 100, 200, 400, 600, 1000 |
| **Chat in Different Rooms** | Participate in unique room conversations     | 💎 50, 100, 200, 400        |
| **Chat with Shapes**        | Have conversations with different Shapes     | 💎 50, 100, 200, 300, 500   |
| **Create Shapes**           | Build your own custom Shapes                 | 💎 100, 200, 300            |
| **Daily Check-In**          | Return each day for bonus rewards (Days 1–5) | 💎 50, 150, 400, 900, 2000  |
| **Leave a Review**          | Share feedback in the Reviews Room           | 💎 200                      |

_<sup>Keep in mind, these numbers can change if anything ever starts to feel out of balance.</sup>_

<Tip>
  Each achievement resets weekly so that you have more chances to earn diamonds to get more prizes.
</Tip>
