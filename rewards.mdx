## How It Works

Rewards give you gems for real activity on Shapes. Finish a task, open Rewards, and tap the gem amount to claim it.

Your current task unlocks the next level after you claim it. Some shop items need a certain level before you can buy them.

New accounts may see a temporary 3x gem boost during their first few hours.

## Rewards Tabs

- **Rewards:** your level tasks and claimable gems.
- **Shop:** Shape Credits and customization rewards you can buy with gems.
- **Gifts:** redeem gem codes, create gem gift codes, and check gift history.

## Gifts

You can send gem gifts from a chat. Direct gifts go to one selected participant in that chat. Chat drops split gems across eligible participants in the chat.

| Gift | Cost | Level | How It Works |
| --- | ---: | ---: | --- |
| Bag of Gems | 500 gems | 23 | Send 500 gems to one person. |
| Chest of Gems | 1,000 gems | 23 | Send 1,000 gems to one person. |
| Gem Grenade | 1,000 gems | 29 | Drop 1,000 gems across chat participants. |
| Gem Airstrike | 5,000 gems | 29 | Drop 5,000 gems across chat participants. |

The Gifts tab also lets you redeem a gem code or create one to share. You choose how many people can redeem it, then choose either **500 gems each** or **1,000 gems each**. The cost is the total gems you put into the code.

## Notes

Rewards are for real participation. If you spam or farm tasks, you can lose gems and task progress.

Rewards, prices, stock, and task requirements can change.

You can earn Shape Credits for free by completing rewards and redeeming credit packs when they are available.
