---
title: "How to Create a Fandom Chat"
description: "Ever wanted to create a chat for a specific fandom? Here's how to get started!"
---

1. **Pick a Fandom**\
   Choose a fandom you’re passionate about or one that’s currently trending.
   - Trending fandoms can be discovered on platforms like Tumblr, Ao3, Google Trends, Netflix, Amazon Prime, HBO Max, Crunchyroll, and more.
2. **Create a New Chat**\
   Start a fresh chat for your fandom.
3. **Add Shapes**\
   Bring in Shapes relevant to your fandom:
   - Use **Tag Search** with the fandom’s name (e.g., “Undertale”) to find related Shapes.
   - Use **Name Search** for specific characters (e.g., search “Sans” to find all Sans Shapes).
   - Test the Shape out - make sure it is responding the way you'd want the Shape to respond. If it's not high quality, move on! 
   - If an important character doesn’t have a high-quality Shape, you can **create one** here: [Shapes Creation](https://shapes.inc/create).
4. **Adjust the Chat Appearance**\
   Customize your chat to match the fandom’s style:
   - Set a relevant **Avatar, Banner, and Background**.
   - Adjust **bubble colors**, add a **description**, and fine-tune other visual elements.
   - Learn more here: [Chat Appearance Guide](https://docs.shapes.inc/chat-appearance).
5. **Configure AI Settings**\
   Make your chat welcoming and interactive:
   - Set **welcome messages and designate a Welcome Shape**. This is found in the AI Configurations.
   - Adjust **custom instructions** in the AI Configurations to guide Shape behavior for your chat.
