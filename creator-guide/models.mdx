## Model Performance Tips

<Tip>
**Temperature Settings**: Some models like Mistral NeMo work best with specific temperature settings (around 0.8) for optimal performance.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Fallback Options**: Consider setting up fallback models like **GPT 4o Mini** or **Gemini Flash 2 (Lite)** in case your primary model goes offline.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Multilingual Support**: For non-English Shapes, **Gemini Flash 2 (Lite)** and **GPT-4.1** offer excellent multilingual capabilities.
</Tip>

<Warning>
Some models like **DeepSeek R1** are uncensored and may produce content without typical safety filters. Use these models responsibly.
</Warning>
