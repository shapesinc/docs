title: "How to make a Shape"
description: "The all-in-one where we'll show you how to make a shape and more!"
---

When it's your first time creating a shape, you'll be asked to log in if you haven't already. After that, you're ready to create your first shape\!

Simply click on the "Create Shape" button and fill out the minimum requirements under "Basic" on the Shape's Personality page and click on the "Create" button.
![Create Shape](/images/create_a_shape.png)
**BOOM\!** You've created your shape\!
![Shape Created](/images/shape_created.png)

<Info>
  If you are having trouble signing in please read How to login to Shapes first, if none of those methods work please email [hi@shapes.inc](mailto:hi@shapes.inc).
</Info>

Once you've created your shape, you'll be redirected to the Shape's profile page. From here you can edit your shape's personality, add Profile Picture, Banner, and more by visiting relevant tabs on the left or [Sidebar](/your-shapes.mdx).

Checkout the [Navigation](/your-shapes.mdx) for more information on how to manage your shapes.
