Shape Premium has been updated to provide a clearer and more consistent experience. We've streamlined how credits work to make premium management easier.

## **What's Changed**

### **Account-Level Credits**
Account credits let you talk to your shapes on premium in any room. When you have account-level premium, shapes will always use a premium engine.

### **Room-Level Credits**
Room credits let you enable premium for just one room, great if you want to share a premium experience with your friends in a room.

**Note:** We've merged individual shape credits into these two options to make premium management clearer and more consistent. If you already have shape-level credits, don't worry — your balance is still available and you can continue using your remaining credits.

## **Premium Features**

With premium credits, you get access to:

- Access to premium engines such as Claude 3.7, 3.5, DeepSeek R1, V3, OpenAI O3-mini, and more!
- Access to premium engine presets from creators (subscription feature)
- Access to upgraded image generation engines
- Voice messaging if enabled by creators (subscription feature)
- Web browsing through your shapes (subscription feature)
