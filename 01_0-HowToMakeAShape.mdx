![Log In Pn](/images/LogIn.png)

1. **Log into** [**shapes.inc**](http://shapes.inc)

   If you previously had logged in with discord you can log in via email by using the same email you used to create your Discord. We'll pull it up and reconnect you to your shapes\!

<Info>
  If you are having trouble signing in please read How to login to Shapes first, if none of those methods work please email [hi@shapes.inc](mailto:hi@shapes.inc).
</Info>

![Create A Shape 1 Pn](/images/CreateAShape_1.png)

2. **Click on "Create A Shape"**

![Create A Shape 2 Pn](/CreateAShape_3.png)

3. **Fill out the minimum requirements under "Basic" on the Shape's Personality page**
   - Choose a name for your Shape
   - Short backstory describing your Shape
   - Response style
   - **Click "Create"**

![Create A Shape 4 Pn](/images/CreateAShape_4.png)

4. **Congratulations\! You're Shape is ready to chat\!**
