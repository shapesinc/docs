**Shape Credits** are our platform’s virtual currency, used to access premium AI engines. They aren’t a 1:1 replacement for technical tokens — instead, they provide a **simplified, user-friendly way** to interact with powerful models without worrying about complex token math.

Each credit grants a **set amount of access** depending on the model you’re using. Different AI engines have different compute costs — and premium models like Grok or Claude 3.7 can cost **5–10x more to operate** than others.

Our credit system reflects these real-world differences, and we’ve designed it to be **fair, and balanced** — not to “drain” your credits unfairly. Credits are charged at each model’s list rate. Actual usage may vary depending on the engine selected and the configuration of your Shape. You can visit our [Shape Credits Guide](https://wiki.shapes.inc/new-to-shapes-guide/products-and-services/what-are-shape-credits/shape-credits-guide) to see an example of differences between engines.

<Info>
  If a user has a premium subscription and Shape Credits, they are consumed in a round robin fashion. This means for each message using a premium engine— we randomly pick one of the available non zero shape engine credit sources.
</Info>

## **Shape Credits are available as a pay-as-you-go system**

**Purchasing Shape Credits** gives you the flexibility to access premium engines through a **pay-as-you-go** system, helping you control your spending without committing to a subscription.

This is **different from premium subscriptions**, which expire monthly and include additional benefits such as:

- Premium engine presets
- Voice capabilities
- `!web` browsing

Shape Credits **never expire** and can be used in the following ways:

- **Your Account (all shapes)** – Use credits across any shape you interact with
- **Your Account (single shape)** – Allocate credits to one specific bot
- **Sponsor a Shape (for all its users)** – All users of that bot receive premium engine benefits
- Ability to gain access to premium engines but not premium engine presets crafted by creator
- Ability to gain access to premium image engines if enabled by the creator of the shape

The number of credits used per message varies depending on the AI model and configuration, since some engines (like GPT-4 or Claude) are significantly more expensive to run than others.

Shape Credits allow you to access these models on demand, giving you flexible access without a subscription commitment.
