**Shape Credits** are our platform's virtual currency, used to access premium AI engines. They aren't a 1:1 replacement for technical tokens — instead, they provide a **simplified, user-friendly way** to interact with powerful models without worrying about complex token math.

Each credit grants a **set amount of access** depending on the model you're using. Different AI engines have different compute costs — and premium models like Claude 3.7 can cost **5–10x more to operate** than others.

Our credit system reflects these real-world differences, and we've designed it to be **fair, and balanced** — not to "drain" your credits unfairly. Credits are charged at each model's list rate. Actual usage may vary depending on the engine selected and the configuration of your Shape. You can visit our [Shape Credits Guide](https://wiki.shapes.inc/new-to-shapes-guide/products-and-services/what-are-shape-credits/shape-credits-guide) to see an example of differences between engines.

## **Shape Credits are available as a pay-as-you-go system**

**Purchasing Shape Credits** gives you the flexibility to access premium engines through a **pay-as-you-go** system.

Shape Credits **never expire** and can be used in the following ways:

## **How Credits Work**

### **Account Credits**

- **Your Account (all shapes)** – Use credits to chat with any shape in any room
- When you have account premium, shapes will always use their best engines
- Works with Auto Top-Up so you never run out

### **Room Credits**

- **Sponsor a Room** – Give everyone in a room premium access
- Perfect for sharing premium with friends in your favorite rooms
- Everyone in that room gets to use premium engines

### **Individual Shape Credits (if you have them)**

- If you bought credits for individual shapes before, you can still use them
- They work with those specific shapes just like before
- **Note:** We're not selling individual shape credits anymore - just account and room credits now

## **What You Get Access To**

With Shape Credits, you get:

- Access to premium engines 
- Access to premium image engines if enabled by the creator of the shape

## **How It Works**

- If you have premium credits but the shape doesn't have a premium engine picked by its creator, it'll automatically use Claude Sonnet 4.5 (one of the best!)
- Right now, if  you want to use free engines with a shape, you'll need to toggle on the option to not use your account credits in the personal AI preferences menu. This can be found by clicking on your room title. For room credits, Shapes will be auto-upgraded to premium.

The number of credits used per message varies depending on the AI model and configuration, since some engines (like GPT-4 or Claude) are significantly more expensive to run than others.

Shape Credits allow you to access these models on demand, giving you flexible access without a subscription commitment.
