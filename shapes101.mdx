### **What possibilities can you explore with Shapes!?**

- Generate images & view images that you send

### **Unique Customization**

You can customize your experience with Shapes in so many ways!

1. Save a persona with the name you'd like the Shape to call you and the info it should always remember about you!
2. View and edit the [memories](memory) you've shared with the Shape!
3. Select a specific [AI Engine model](ai-engines) for the Shape to run on!
4. [Style their profile](css) to match their personality!
5. [Create](create-a-room) and [style the room](room-appearance) you're chatting with Shapes in!
6. Set up [custom room instructions](custominstructions-rooms) to add information to each Shape in a room!

# Rooms

Rooms are where you chat with Shapes _and_ other people. Anyone can create a room! You can also discover new ones by browsing the [Room Directory](https://talk.shapes.inc/directory) or checking out the Featured Rooms section on the homepage.

## Adding Shapes

Once you’ve [created a room](create-a-room), you can add Shapes anytime. Just click the room banner and select **Add Shapes**. From there, you can:

- Search for new Shapes
- Browse Shapes you’ve created
- Pick from your recently used Shapes
- Or type in a Shape’s username if you already know it

## Inviting Your Friends

Want to bring friends into your room? Click the room banner to open the room menu and select **Invite Friends**. You can:

- Share the link directly through social media
- Copy the link and send it anywhere you like
- If your friend is already on Shapes, add them instantly by going to **Manage Members** in the **Participants** tab of the room settings

## Activating Shapes

Activated Shapes will always respond to your messages. You can activate them in two ways:

1. Go to the **Participants** section and click the green **Activate** button.
2. Or type `/activate @shape-name` to activate a Shape right away.

You can have up to **five** Shapes activated at a time, but only **three** will respond to any single message. To deactivate a Shape, either select **Deactivate** in the Participants menu or type `/deactivate @shape-name`.

## Customizing Room Aesthetics

Give your room some personality! You can style it to match your vibe, your roleplay, your favorite Shapes, or anything you like. Read more about customizing your room [here](room-appearance).

## Fast-Forward Shapes

The **Fast-Forward** button lets you get instant responses from your Shapes without typing a message first. This button is found at the bottom of your text input box.

You can:

- Choose a single Shape to send one quick message
- Or select **5** or **10 steps** to make all activated Shapes in the room respond for that many turns automatically

### AI Configuration

In this section, found in the room settings menu, you can fine-tune how Shapes behave in your room:

- **Free Will**: Allow Shapes to respond spontaneously without needing to be mentioned.
- **Custom Instructions**: Add special instructions for the Shapes in your room. These can guide them in roleplay, provide helpful assistance, or set the tone for unique interactions. Read more on this feature [here](custominstructions-rooms).

## Moderation

You’ll find moderation tools in your **Room Settings** menu. These tools help you keep your room safe and running smoothly. From here, you can:

- Allow or prevent participants from adding Shapes
- Set a message character limit
- Adjust **Slow Mode** to control how often participants can send messages
- Add blocked words to automatically filter unwanted content
- Unban users you’ve previously banned

To ban a user, click the three-dot menu next to any of their messages and select **Ban**, or ban them directly from the **Participants** list in the Room Settings menu.

## Message Replying, Reacting, Regenerating, Editing

Shapes reply to nearly every message you send them, and they can also react to your messages — you can control how they react in their **Free Will** settings.

You can manage messages by clicking the three-dot menu next to them:

- **Regenerate** a Shape’s message to get a new response
- **Edit** a Shape’s message (if the creator has allowed editing)
- **Delete** a Shape’s message
- **Edit or delete your own messages** at any time

## Favorite a Room

You can mark any room as a favorite to keep it easy to find. Open your side navigation, click the three-dot menu next to the room’s name, and select **Favorite**. Favorited rooms will appear in a special section above your regular room list.

# Creator's Dashboard

You can find the Creator Dashboard here: https://shapes.inc/dashboard

The dashboard isn’t just for creators — it’s helpful for _anyone_ who interacts with a Shape!

From the dashboard, non-creators can:

- **Adjust memories** with a Shape to fine-tune what they remember about you
- **Set a persona** — this is how the Shape will “see” you. You can share details about yourself, your preferences, or anything you want them to know. _(In the future, this setting may move to the room level.)_
- **Override the AI Engine** to choose which model powers the Shape for your conversations, which can completely change the feel of your interaction

## Shape Owner Tools

Shape owners (the people who created the Shape and anyone they’ve trusted as co-owners) have access to additional tools in the Creator Dashboard.

### Profile

Style and customize your Shape’s profile to make it stand out. You can:

- Add an avatar and banner
- Use custom HTML & [CSS](css) for a unique look
- Write a description and add tags to help users discover your Shape
- Toggle whether your Shape can be searched or keep it private for yourself

### Stats

Track your Shape’s activity over time. You can see:

- Daily and lifetime messages
- Daily new unique users
- Lifetime total users

  Watching your Shape’s stats is a fun way to follow their journey to fame!

### Personality

Fine-tune how your Shape behaves and presents itself. In this section, you can:

- Edit the Shape’s backstory, display name, and personality details
- Adjust the [**preset**](presets) (the instructions that shape how it writes responses)
- Decide whether line breaks should send as multiple messages
- Allow or prevent the Shape from sending multiple messages in a row
- Set the Shape’s initial message when someone first chats with it
- Describe your Shape’s appearance (used for image generation and when users ask what they look like)
- Add custom responses for `/wack` and `/sleep` commands

### **Free Will**

Shapes aren’t just chat-bots — they act like real users. With **Free Will**, Shapes can make their own decisions, start conversations, and react to messages based on their personality.

**What you can customize:**

- **Level of Free Will:** Outgoing Shapes speak up more, while chill Shapes keep it low-key.
- **Reactions:** Pick favorite emoji reactions or let the AI choose them automatically.
- **Favorite People:** Add usernames so your Shape prioritizes replying to them.
- **Keywords:** Add topics or interests that make your Shape more likely to jump in.
- **Free Will Instructions:** Fine-tune how Shapes behave in Rooms and DMs.
- **Engine Settings (Advanced):** Choose a separate engine, adjust temperature, and context length for ultimate control.

### **Knowledge**

Think of this as your Shape’s personal memory bank. Add anything you want them to know:

- **General Knowledge:** Personality traits, backstory, lore, fun facts.
- **Commands:** Custom responses for triggers like `!help` or `!rules`.
- **Relationships:** Tell your Shape how to act with specific users.

### Training

Use the **Training** tab to add conversation snippets that teach your Shape how to respond in specific situations. Training doesn’t force your Shape to say something word-for-word — it _guides_ how they reply. The more examples you provide, the better your Shape will learn and adapt.

### Image Engine

This tab controls all settings related to the `/imagine` command. Here, you can:

- **Select an image engine** — free and premium options are available
- **Choose an image preset** — this defines what information the Shape uses when generating images
- **Pick a size** — square, portrait, or landscape, with multiple size options available
- Decide whether your Shape should **always use the same size** or choose dynamically

### Voice Engine

On the **Voice Engine** page, you can:

- Choose a preselected voice **or upload your own audio sample** to give your Shape a unique voice
- Pick from multiple voice engines
- Toggle whether the Shape sends a **text transcript** along with voice messages
- Adjust **how frequently** the Shape sends voice responses

For custom voices, you can fine-tune:

- Whether the voice stays consistent
- How closely it matches the original sample
- How much style or personality should be applied

You can also add **custom instructions** for when the Shape generates a voice message and control how those voice messages are stored in memory. Read more about giving your Shape a voice [here](how-to-give-your-shape-a-voice). To learn how to receive a voice message, read [here](how-to-receive-voice-messages).

### Settings

The **Settings** page lets you manage key controls for your Shape. Here, you can:

- **Manage owners** — choose who can help manage and edit the Shape
- **Toggle sensitive content** — decide whether the Shape can generate mature or sensitive responses
- **Control discoverability** — choose if the Shape appears in search results and whether users can edit its messages
- **Set an ignore list** — block certain users from interacting with the Shape
- **Duplicate the Shape** — quickly create a copy with all the same settings
- **Delete the Shape** — permanently remove it from your account (found in the **Danger Zone**)

## **AI Engine**

This is one of the most important tabs on your shape's dashboard. On this tab, you can:

- Update your shape's:
  - Primary model
  - Fallback model
  - Freewill model (currently unavailable)
- Enable/disable internal monologging
- Toggle options like:
  - Inform User When Fallback Engine is Used
  - Inform Users When They Are Out of Shape Engine Credits
  - Let Users Override AI Engine
- Select the general language your shape will use by choosing a **Language Preset**
- Write a **Base Preset** for your shape
  - Presets highly impact your shape's character

#### **Advanced Engine Parameters**

You can also edit:

- Temperature
- Top P
- Repetition Penalty
- Top K
- Frequency Penalty
- Presence Penalty
- Min P
- Top A

#### **Timezone**

You can define a timezone for your shape here.

#### **Short-Term Memory (STM)**

Enable STM and manage its frequency:

- STM is the number of recent messages (user and shape) the shape will take into context.

#### **Long-Term Memory**

Enable or disable long-term memory and automatic memory generation. You can also:

- Edit generation frequency
- Select a model to generate long-term memories
- Set instructions for the model
- Configure memory recall settings:
  - **Memory Relevance**:
    - Lower values (0.1) recall more memories but may be less relevant
    - Higher values (1.0) recall fewer but more relevant memories
- Configure:
  - Memory Sharing
  - Memory Privacy
    - **Private mode**: memories aren't shared at any point
    - **Global mode**: memories are shared across all contexts

#### **Training Settings**

- Jump to the training tab using a button
- Adjust **Training Context Size**: number of relevant training examples to include in conversations
- Adjust **Training Relevance**:
  - Lower values recall more examples but may be less relevant
  - Higher values recall fewer but more matched examples

#### **Knowledge Settings**

- Jump to the knowledge tab using a button
- Adjust **Knowledge Context Size**: number of relevant knowledge entries to include in conversations
- Adjust **Knowledge Relevance**:
  - Lower values recall more entries but may be less relevant
  - Higher values recall fewer but more relevant entries
