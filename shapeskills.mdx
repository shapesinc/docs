This guide walks you through:

- How to enable and set up skills
- How to add a custom MCP skill (example: memes)
- Built-in skills available by default
- Tips for getting the best results

It's really magical to see a Shape proactively use a tool based on their mood, preferences, and personality.

## 1. Enabling Shape Skills

To start, you'll need either:

- A **room** that you own, or (see: [how to create a room](create-a-room))
- A **DM** with your Shape

Once that's ready:

1. Open **Room Settings**
2. Go to the **AI Configurations** tab
3. Toggle on **Shape Skills**

👉 Each skill is managed separately. You decide how many (or how few) your Shape can use.

![Aiconfigmenu Pn](/images/aiconfigmenu.png)

## 2. Adding Custom MCP Skills

Scroll down in AI Configurations until you see **Custom Skills**. Toggle this on to add a new skill.

Custom Skills support MCP servers with API keys from [**smithery.ai**](http://smithery.ai) or other MCP providers.

With smithery, you can connect a wide variety of tools, such as:

- Image, video, or music generation
- GitHub integration
- Web scraping
- Spotify playlist creation
- So many more possibilities...

Even if you usually roleplay with your Shape, adding a skill can unlock new, fun interactions (like asking them to share their favorite YouTube video).

![Simplemcpaction Pn](/images/simplemcpaction.png)

The Endpoint URL you will get from [_smithery.ai_](http://smithery.ai) or other MCP providers.

Example: Adding a Meme Generator (Imgflip)

1. Go to [smithery.ai](http://smithery.ai) and browse available MCPs
2. Create a **Smithery account**.
3. Search for **Meme**
4. Select **Meme Creation Platform**
5. Create an **Imgflip account** and connect it to smithery
6. Click **"Get URL with keys instead"** and copy the link
7. Back in Shapes, paste this link into your **AI Configuration** settings under Custom Skills
8. Name your skill (e.g., _Imgflip Memes_)

Your Shape now has access to the meme generator! 🎉

![Smitherygeturl Pn](/images/Smitherygeturl.png)

The yellow box is highlighting where to find the URL that you need to share with Shapes.

## 3. Triggering Skills in Chat

Different AI engines handle tool use differently. For best results, we currently recommend:

- **Grok Engines**
- **Claude Engines**

(Example: Grok 3 Mini is free and works well with Shape skills.)

Once your skill is enabled, you can use **natural language** to interact with it. For example:

- “Make me a meme about Mondays”
- “Can you generate a random meme for this conversation?”

Your Shape may pull directly from the tool, improvise based on context, or mix in its personality.

Sometimes, a Shape can proactively decide to use a tool!

## 4. Built-in Skills

Shapes also comes with **pre-input skills** ready to use out of the box. These include:

- **Weather** lookup
- **Hacker News** updates
- **Reddit search**
- **Stuff To Do** (scheduled skills)
- **Total Recall** (memory system)
- **Voice generation**
- **YouTube search**

  <Info>
    You must have one of the basic skills selected in order for your shape to use a custom skill. This enables them to see their tools.
  </Info>

## 5. Tips & Tricks

- **Keep it clear**: Give your skill a descriptive name so you know what it does later.
- **Experiment**: Not every engine uses skills the same way; try switching if something feels off.
- **Mix it up**: Use skills in both casual chats and roleplay to discover fun new dynamics.
- This is an **Experimental Feature**! Some integrations might not work as expected.

# Skill Ideas:

## 🎨 Creative Fun

- **Image generation** → Ask your Shape to create concept art, avatars, or illustrations on the fly.
- **Video generation** → Have your Shape generate short clips or animations to match a roleplay scene.
- **Music generation** → Get background tracks or songs made for your character, story, or mood.

<Warning>
  _AI generation MCPs (such as video, audio, images) will often require a paid API key from the provider, such as Google or OpenAI. Shapes does not provide these._
</Warning>

### 📚 Learning & Knowledge

- **Wikipedia summaries** → Ask your Shape to grab quick facts mid-conversation.
- **Language translation** → Have Shapes instantly translate or teach words in another language.
- **Math solver** → Turn your Shape into a problem-solving study buddy.
- **News feeds** → Stay updated with the latest headlines from your favorite sources.
- **Scholarly Articles** → Connect your Shapes to sources like ArXiv to do deep research & learn new things.
- **Learn Coding** → Connect your Shapes to MCPs where you can learn Python or other coding languages.

### 🌍 Everyday Life

- **Weather forecast** → Ask your Shape if you need an umbrella before heading out.
- **To-do lists & reminders** → Shapes can manage your daily tasks like a personal assistant.
- **Recipe finder** → Ask a Shape what you can cook with the ingredients in your fridge.

### 🕹️ Entertainment & Hobbies

- **YouTube search** → Have Shapes pull videos to match their mood.
- **Spotify playlist generator** → Get a playlist built around your conversation or vibe.
- **Meme generators** → Auto-generate memes to spice up roleplays or group chats.

### 💼 Productivity

- **GitHub integration** → Let your Shape check issues, pull requests, or repos.
- **Notion or Google Docs** → Generate, organize, or update notes while chatting.
- **Web scraping** → Have Shapes pull info from a site so you don't have to leave the chat.
- **Agentic tasks** → Get Shapes to chain skills, like researching a topic and then drafting a summary.

### 🏡 Lifestyle & Exploration

- **Real estate listings (MLS)** → Have a Shape fetch all of the fresh real estate listings in your area each morning.
- **Travel planning** → Get flights, hotels, and local attraction suggestions.
- **Fitness tools** → Have Shapes create workout routines or track progress.
- **Shopping assistants** → Compare prices or find product recommendations.

👉 The magic is that you can mix these into roleplay, assistant-style use, or just casual chats. For example:

- Your roleplay Shape could pull up a playlist to match the scene.
- A study buddy Shape could fetch real-time info from Wikipedia while tutoring you.
- A personal assistant Shape could generate a workout plan and then remind you of tomorrow’s schedule.
