### How Friends Work

You make friends on Shapes by following people.

If you follow someone and they follow you back, you become friends.

![Friends Pn](/images/friends.png)

### Following Someone

To follow somebody:

1. Open a chat.
2. Click the person's avatar.
3. Click the heart icon on their profile.

Shapes will let you know that you are now following them. If they follow you back, they will become your friend.

### Checking Followers

To see who follows you:

1. Click **Search** in the sidebar.
2. Select the **Followers** option.

You can also use the **Following** option to see people you follow.
