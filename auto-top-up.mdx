### **How Does It Work**

- **Auto Top-Up is on by default** — if you have less than 100 credits and a payment method saved, it will automatically renew for \$19.99 (1,999 credits)
- This ensures uninterrupted access without worrying about encountering fallbacks
- **Auto Top-Up only works for account credits** and won't affect any old shape credits you might have
- You can manage this in your **Payment History** (formerly called "Access Billing Portal")

### **Managing Auto Top-Up**

- To turn off Auto Top-Up, remove your payment method in Payment History
- You can update or remove payment methods anytime
- If you prefer to add credits manually, simply remove your saved payment method
