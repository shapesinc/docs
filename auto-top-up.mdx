### **How Does It Work**

- As soon as your balance hits 50 or below, we'll automatically refill your credits, so you can stay focused on chatting, not checking out.
- The amount of shape credits are based off your most recent previous purchase. For example: You pay \$4.99 and then opt-in for auto top-up, the next time your account hits 50 or below credits, you'll automatically be charged the same amount.
- This auto top-up feature only pops up after purchasing shape credits.
- You can manage this subscription in your billing portal, which you can now easily access at the bottom of the premium landing page.
