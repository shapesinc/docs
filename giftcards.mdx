Now you can share your love of shapes with others by purchasing gift cards! Shape Gift Cards allow users to give the gift of our service to friends and family by pre-purchasing credit that can be redeemed for Shape specific subscriptions or features. You can find this available on your premium dashboard.

<Info>
  If you need help purchasing a gift card, visit [How to Purchase a Shapes Gift Card](/purchasegiftcards)
</Info>

Click 'Premium' button to access your premium dashboard..

![Premium Page Pn](/images/PremiumPage.png)

### **Important Details About Purchasing Gift Cards**

1. You must have an account with us in order to make any purchases
2. Gift card amounts are in increments made to match the pricing for the products we offer. (i.e. - Shape Engine Credits or Premium)
3. We offer you a customizable gift card should you exceed the amount we offer. The lowest minimum amount available to gift is at \$4.99.

### **Important Details About Using Gift Cards**

1. Once gift cards are purchased or redeemed we are not able to provide refunds.
2. Redeemed Gift Cards transfer and turn into Credit Balances. You can review your credit balance under Redeem Gift Card.

   ![Giftcards2 Pn](/images/giftcards2.png)
3. Currently gifting subscriptions directly and to enable using multiple payment methods for a single purchase are not available. Credit Balances cannot be used to purchase gift cards.
4. Gift Card Credit Balances are made to be used to pay for a product in full with a single payment. Meaning users cannot split payment between credit balance and a credit card.
   - ❗ **For subscriptions:** When using gift cards for a premium **subscription**, you'll still need to provide **credit card** information at checkout (required for ongoing subscription service)
   - 👍 You can cancel your subscription anytime through your Payment History under ['Active Subscriptions'](https://shapes.inc/premium?section=purchase_history) on your premium dashboard
