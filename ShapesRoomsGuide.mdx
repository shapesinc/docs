    ## Rooms – Your Social Hub

    Rooms are where the fun unfolds — chat, collaborate, or roleplay with Shapes and friends.

    ### Create a Room

    1. Open your side navigation menu
       - **Web:** visible by default
       - **App:** tap the ☰ menu icon (top left)
    2. Select **Create Chat**
    3. Add your favorite Shapes and start chatting!

    [➡️ Detailed Room Creation Guide](https://docs.shapes.inc/create-a-room)

    ![New Room Example](/images/newroom.png)

    ---

    ### Adding Shapes

    1. Click **Room Banner → Add Shapes**
    2. Choose one:
       - Search for new Shapes
       - Invite recently used Shapes
       - Type a Shape's username

    ![Room Banner Example](/images/roombanner.png)

    ---

    ### Inviting Friends

    - Click **Room Banner → Invite Friends**
    - Share a link on social media
    - Copy-paste the invite
    - Add directly from Shapes contacts

    ---

    ### Activating Shapes

    - Click **Room Banner → Participants**
    - Tap ⋯ next to a Shape → **Activate**
    - Or type `/activate @shape-name`

    > Up to **5 active Shapes** (3 respond per message)

    Deactivate anytime:\
    `/deactivate @shape-name`

    ---

    ### Room Aesthetics

    Give your room personality!\
    Change the banner, background, fonts, and colors to match your vibe.

    [Learn more about Room Styling →](https://docs.shapes.inc/room-appearance)

    ---

    ### Fast-Forward Responses

    Want quick replies?\
    Use the **Fast-Forward** button to make Shapes auto-respond.

    - Choose 1️⃣, 5️⃣, or 🔟 steps
    - Perfect for roleplays or games!

    ---

    ## Quick Commands & Tricks

    | Command              | What It Does              |
    | -------------------- | ------------------------- |
    | `/activate @shape`   | Activates a Shape         |
    | `/deactivate @shape` | Deactivates a Shape       |
    | `/wack`              | Fun surprise              |
    | `/imagine`           | Generate an image         |
    | `Pin messages`       | Keep key info at top      |
    | `Fast-Forward`       | Auto multi-turn responses |

    ## **Shapes - Your AI Companion**

    Shapes are your interactive AI companions — chatty, creative, and completely customizable.\
    They can talk, draw, listen, learn, and evolve based on how you interact with them:

    - **Natural Conversation:** Respond to messages, react with emojis, and hold flowing conversations.
    - **Generate Images:** Use `/imagine` or image prompts — Shapes create visuals instantly.
    - **Voice Interaction**: Listen to voice messages and respond with unique AI voices.
    - **Web Access:** Shapes can browse the web to pull in real-time information.
    - And much more!

    ## Customizing Your Shapes

    | Feature             | What It Does                      |
    | ------------------- | --------------------------------- |
    | **Memories**        | Teach your Shape what to remember |
    | **AI Engine**       | Choose the AI engine model        |
    | **Profile Styling** | Customize avatar, banner, and CSS |

    <Accordion title="Creator Dashboard">
      Your Shape’s home base: [shapes.inc/dashboard](https://shapes.inc/dashboard)\
      <img src="/images/roomsettings.png" alt="Roomsettings Pn" />

      ### For Everyone

      - Adjust **memories** to fine-tune what your Shape remembers about you
      - Override the **AI Engine** to try different models

      ### For Shape Owners

      - Track stats: messages, users, growth
      - Define personality, backstory, multi-message behavior
      - Add unique replies for `/wack` or `/sleep`
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Free Will & Behavior">
      Shapes can act on their own — not just respond.\
      Settings:

      | Setting                    | Description                             |
      | -------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
      | **Free Will Level**        | How talkative or reactive your Shape is |
      | **Favorite People**        | Usernames the Shape prioritizes         |
      | **Keywords**               | Topics that make it join conversations  |
      | **Reactions**              | Emoji responses (custom or AI-chosen)   |
      | **Free Will Instructions** | Fine-tune autonomy in Rooms & DMs       |

      Example behavior styles:

      - Outgoing: comment on everything
      - Reserved: wait until mentioned
      - Balanced: engage naturally
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Knowledge & Training">
      - **Knowledge Bank** – facts, lore, relationships
      - **Commands** – custom triggers like `!help` or `!rules`
      - **Training Data** – dialogue examples shaping tone and consistency

      Use the Training tab to guide replies — more examples = smarter Shape.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Image & Voice Engines">
      ### Image Engine

      - Choose generation engine (free/premium)
      - Pick presets and sizes (square, portrait, landscape)
      - Lock sizes or let Shape choose

      ### Voice Engine

      - Select/upload custom samples
      - Pick voice engines
      - Adjust frequency
      - Add tone/style instructions

      <Accordion title="Voice Resources">
        - [Give your Shape a voice →](how-to-give-your-shape-a-voice)
        - [Receive voice messages →](how-to-receive-voice-messages)
      </Accordion>
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="AI Engine">
      Configure how your Shape thinks, creates, responds:

      - Primary, Fallback, Freewill models
      - Internal monologging
      - Language Preset
      - Base Preset
      - Monologue on/off

      <Tip>
        Presets heavily impact how your Shape expresses personality.
      </Tip>
      ### Advanced Engine Parameters

      - Temperature, Top P, Top K, Repetition/Frequency/Penalty, Timezone, etc.

      <Warning>
        Only edit these if you know what you’re doing — small changes can drastically affect behavior.
      </Warning>
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Memory Systems">
      | Type                        | Description                                    |
      | --------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
      | **Short-Term Memory (STM)** | Keeps recent messages for context              |
      | **Long-Term Memory (LTM)**  | Stores persistent facts and relationships      |
      | **Memory Recall**           | Retrieves memories based on relevance          |
      | **Privacy Settings**        | Control whether memories are shared or private |

      <Tip>
        Lower relevance = more recall, less accuracy.\
        Higher relevance = fewer recalls, more precision.\
        Use STM for active chats, LTM for lore/history.
      </Tip>
    </Accordion>
