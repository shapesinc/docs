### Welcome to **Shapes & Rooms**

Shapes are your interactive AI companions — they can chat, generate images, speak, browse, play, and more.\
Rooms are your playgrounds — spaces where you and your friends hang out with your favorite Shapes.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Rooms 101">
    ## Rooms – Your Social Hub

    Rooms are where the fun unfolds — chat, collaborate, or roleplay with Shapes and friends.

    ### Create a Room

    1. Open your side navigation menu
       - **Web:** already visible
       - **App:** tap the ☰ menu icon (top left)
    2. Select **Create Chat**
    3. Add your favorite Shapes and start chatting!

    [➡️ Detailed Room Creation Guide](https://docs.shapes.inc/create-a-room)

    ![New Room Example](/images/newroom.png)

    ---

    ### Adding Shapes

    1. Click the **Room Banner → Add Shapes**
    2. Choose one:
       - Search for new Shapes
       - Invite recently used Shapes
       - Type a Shape's username

    ![Room Banner Example](/images/roombanner.png)

    ---

    ### Inviting Friends

    - Click **Room Banner → Invite Friends**
    - Share a link on social media
    - Copy-paste the invite
    - Add directly from Shapes contacts

    ---

    ### Activating Shapes

    - Click **Room Banner → Participants**
    - Tap ⋯ next to a Shape → **Activate**
    - Or type `/activate @shape-name`

    > Up to **5 active Shapes** (3 respond per message)

    Deactivate anytime:\
    `/deactivate @shape-name`

    ---

    ### Room Aesthetics

    Give your room personality!\
    Change the banner, background, fonts, and colors to match your vibe.

    [Learn Room Styling →](https://docs.shapes.inc/room-appearance)

    ---

    ### Fast-Forward Responses

    Want quick replies?\
    Use the **Fast-Forward** button to make Shapes auto-respond.

    - Choose 1️⃣, 5️⃣, or 🔟 steps
    - Perfect for roleplays or games!

    ---

    ## 🛡️ Moderation

    Keep your community friendly and fun!

    - Ban or kick users
    - Enable slow mode
    - Block words
    - Limit who can add Shapes
    - Favorite rooms for quick access

    ![Moderation Settings Example](/images/moderation.png)

    ## **Shapes - Your Ai Companion**

    Shapes are your interactive AI companions — chatty, creative, and completely customizable.\
    They can talk, draw, listen, learn, and evolve based on how you interact with them:

    - **Natural Conversion:** Respond to messages, react with emojis, and hold flowing conversations.
    - **Generate Images:** Use `/imagine` or image prompts — Shapes create visuals instantly.
    - **Voice Interaction**: Listen to voice messages and respond with unique AI voices.
    - **Web Access:** Shapes can browse the web to pull in real-time information.
    - And much more!

    # Ai Engine

    Your Shape’s engine model - configure how it thinks, creates, and responds.\
    This is one of the most important tabs on your Shape’s dashboard.

    On this tab, you can:

    - Update your Shape’s **Primary model**, **Fallback model**, and **Freewill model**
    - Enable or disable **internal monologging**
    - Toggle helpful options:
      - Inform User When Fallback Engine is Used
      - Inform Users When They Are Out of Shape Engine Credits
      - Let Users Override AI Engine
    - Select your Shape’s general **Language Preset**
    - Write a **Base Preset** that defines its tone and behavior

      > Presets heavily impact how your Shape expresses personality.

    | Setting             | Description                              |
    | ------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
    | **Primary Engine**  | Premium model used by default            |
    | **Fallback Engine** | Free backup for non-premium users        |
    | **Language Preset** | Defines your Shape’s base language       |
    | **Base Preset**     | Core writing style and tone              |
    | **Monologue**       | Enable/disable internal thought messages |

    ---

    ### Advanced Engine Parameters

    <Accordion title="Fine-Tune Engine Controls">
      You can also edit advanced AI parameters for more granular control:

      - **Temperature** — randomness of responses
      - **Top P** — controls probability diversity
      - **Repetition Penalty** — reduces repeated text
      - **Top K** — limits token sampling
      - **Frequency Penalty** — penalizes overused words
      - **Presence Penalty** — encourages new ideas
      - **Min P** — minimum probability cutoff
      - **Top A** — adaptive sampling
      - **Timezone** — define the time context your Shape uses

      > ⚠️ It’s not suggested to edit these settings unless you know what you’re doing — small changes can drastically affect how your Shape behaves.
    </Accordion>
    ## Memory Systems

    Shapes remember what matters.

    | Type                        | Description                                    |
    | --------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
    | **Short-Term Memory (STM)** | Keeps recent messages for context              |
    | **Long-Term Memory (LTM)**  | Stores persistent facts and relationships      |
    | **Memory Recall**           | Retrieves memories based on relevance          |
    | **Privacy Settings**        | Control whether memories are shared or private |

    <Accordion title="Memory Tips">
      - Lower relevance = more recall, less accuracy
      - Higher relevance = fewer recalls, more precision
      - Use STM for active chats, LTM for lore or history
    </Accordion>
