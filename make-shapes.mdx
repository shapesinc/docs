Anyone can spin up a Shape in two minutes. Making one that people *keep* talking to is a craft, and it's a fun one to learn.

This is a guide in five chapters. Each one takes your Shape a step further, from a blank page to a character people seek out. Read it start to finish the first time, then come back to any chapter when you need it. (Just want to use Shapes other people made? That's the [Use Shapes](/use-shapes) track.)

## Before you start

You don't need any of this to begin. A name and one good sentence are enough to create a Shape and start talking. Everything else is here for when you want to go further.

When you're ready, two pages do a lot of heavy lifting alongside the chapters: [Prompt Engineering](/prompt-engineering) for writing fields that work, and the [Showcase](/showcase) for complete configs you can copy and tweak.
