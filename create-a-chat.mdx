### How to Create a Chat on Shapes

### Step 1: Log In

Go to [**Shapes.inc**](http://Shapes.inc) and sign in to your account.

### Step 2: Create a Chat

Open your side navigation menu:

- On **web**, this will already be open.
- On the **app**, tap the menu icon in the top left.

From the menu, select **Create Chat**.

To set up your chat, choose the purpose of the chat:

- **Roleplay** – for deep lore and storytelling
- **Casual** – for hanging out
- **Smart** – for productive discussions and getting things done

You can also describe the chat's purpose (optional).

Next, add at least one Shape to the chat. You can:

- Add Shapes from your recent chats (“Friends”)
- Enter a Shape’s username (found on their webpage)
- Select **Random** if you don’t have a specific Shape in mind
- Use the search bar to look up a Shape

Once you’ve chosen your Shape(s), your chat will be created.

### Step 3: Customize Your Chat

After adding a Shape, you’ll see options to personalize your chat:

- **Chat Name** – what your chat will be called
- **Chat Banner** – an image displayed at the top of the chat (optional)
- **Chat Description** – lets others know what your chat is about (optional)
- **Chat Tags** – make it easier for others to discover your chat (optional)

### Step 4: AI Settings (Optional)

AI Settings let you control how Shapes behave in your chat:

- **Chat Preset** – Add special instructions for the Shapes in your chat. These can guide them in roleplay, provide helpful assistance, or set the tone for unique interactions. Read more on this feature [**here**](https://docs.shapes.inc/custominstructions-rooms).
- **Free Will Responses** – allow Shapes to respond spontaneously without needing to be mentioned.
- **Don’t Use My Credits** – make Shapes use a free engine instead of your credits for a premium one.

### Step 5: Invite People (Optional)

You can invite friends in a few ways:

- Enter their usernames during chat creation to add them right away
- Add them later by tapping your chat's title at the top to open the menu
- Share your chat link so others can join

That’s it! Your chat is ready for chatting, collaborating, and building fun moments together.

## More to Know

### Manage Your Chat

Click the chat title at the top to open the chat menu. From here, you can:

- Add or remove Shapes at any time
- Manage permissions for people (click the “...” next to their name; you can even assign them as Chat Owner)
- Scroll down to your Shapes list and **Activate Shapes** to let multiple Shapes reply without needing to be @mentioned

### Pin Important Messages

Use **Pin Messages** in the menu to:

- Highlight favorite or important messages
- Share key links or info for newcomers

## Moderation

Here you can manage important safety and participation settings:

- Allow or prevent other participants from adding Shapes
- Set a message character limit
- Adjust **Slow Mode** to control how frequently participants can send messages
- Add blocked words to filter unwanted content
- Unban any previously banned users from this menu
