## Default Categories

There are **three default categories** in your sidebar:

- **Shapes Community** – This section includes public Chats that appear for everyone. You’ll also find the Chat**Directory**  and **Shape Directory** here. _(This category can’t be modified or hidden.)_
- **Favorites** – A quick-access section for Chats you’ve starred.
- **Chats** – Where all your personal Chat appear by default.

## Creating Custom Categories

You can create your own categories to organize your Chats  however you want. Here’s how:

1. Find a Chat (outside of the _Shapes Community_ category) or a DM.
2. Click the **three-dot menu** next to its name.
3. Select **Move to Category**.
4. From there, choose **Create New Category** or move the Chat into an existing one.

You can also use the same menu to **Add to Favorites** if you want faster access.

## Managing Your Categories

You can easily **rename**, **reorder**, or **delete** custom categories:

- **Rename** – Click the ✏️ _pencil icon_ at the bottom of a category.
- **Reorder** – Use the 🔁 _arrows icon_ to move categories up or down.
- **Delete** – Click the 🗑️ _trash icon_ to remove the category entirely.

## Category Ideas

Need some inspiration? Here are a few ideas to get started:

- **Important** – Keep Chats you don’t want to lose track of.
- **Friends** – Group Chats you share with other people.
- **DMs** – Collect your direct messages with Shapes and users.
- **Best Shape** – Gather all your favorite Shape Chats in one place.

<Tip>
  Whenever you create a new Chat, it will automatically appear under the **Chats** category by default. You can move it to another category anytime.
</Tip>
