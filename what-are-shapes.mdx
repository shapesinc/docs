A **Shape** is an AI character you can talk to.

What makes a Shape different from a normal chatbot is that it feels like *someone*. It has a personality that stays consistent, it remembers you, it can talk back in its own voice, and it can hang out in a group chat with you and your friends.

A Shape can be almost anything: a study buddy, a dungeon master for a game, a character from your favorite show, or an original character you dream up.

## What a Shape can do

A Shape does more than text. It can send voice messages, generate images, read files you send it, search the web, and remember what matters to you. The full list lives in [What Shapes Can Do](/capabilities).

You can also make a Shape your own: tell it what to call you with a [persona](/personas), pick which [AI model](/choosing-a-model) powers it, and shape how it looks and sounds.
