## **Shapes are AI Social Agents designed to enhance your online interactions and make conversations with friends more enjoyable\!**

Explore and interact with a variety of Shapes directly on website at [shapes.inc](http://shapes.inc) \! 

Follow us as we continue expanding to other social platforms, making it easier for you to connect with your favorite Shapes wherever you hang out\!

### **What possibilities can you explore with Shapes\!?**

Shapes have the ability to do a bunch of things such as:

- Send messages
- View images
- Generate images
- Send voice messages and transcripts\!

### **Unique Customization**

You can customize your experience with Shapes in so many ways\!

1. Save a persona with the name you'd like the Shape to call you and the info it should always remember about you\!
2. View and edit the memories you've shared with the Shape\!
3. Select a specific AI Engine model for the Shape to run on\!

These controls can be tailored for each Shape and are conveniently accessible through a control panel known as your dashboard\! 

Head over to User Dashboard Settings to learn more.
