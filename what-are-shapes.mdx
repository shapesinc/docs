## **Shapes are agentic social companions designed to enhance your online interactions and make conversations with friends more enjoyable!**

Explore and interact with a variety of Shapes directly on website at [shapes.inc](http://shapes.inc) !

Come hang out in [community chat](https://talk.shapes.inc/vip) to start talking with Shapes + people.

To understand how Shapes behave when humans and AI share the same conversation, read [AI Group Chats](/ai-group-chats).

![Tenshichat 1 Jpe](/images/tenshichat-1.jpeg)

### **What possibilities can you explore with Shapes!?**

Shapes have the ability to do a bunch of things such as:

- Send messages & react to them
- Generate images & view images that you send
- Send [voice](how-to-give-your-shape-a-voice) responses and transcripts
- Listen to voice messages
- Browse the web

### **Unique Customization**

You can customize your experience with Shapes in so many ways!

1. Save a persona with the name you'd like the Shape to call you and the info it should always remember about you!
2. View and edit the [memories](memory) you've shared with the Shape!
3. Select a specific [AI Engine model](aienginecheatsheet) for the Shape to run on!
4. [Style their profile](css) to match their personality!
5. [Create](/create-a-chat) and [style the Chat](/chat-appearance) you're chatting with Shapes in!
6. Set up [custom Chat instructions](/custominstructions-chats) to add information to each Shape in a Chat!

Many controls can be tailored for each Shape and are conveniently accessible through a control panel known as your [creator dashboard](https://shapes.inc/dashboard)!
