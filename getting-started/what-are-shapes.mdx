# What are Shapes?

Shapes Inc is a platform that lets you create, customize, and interact with AI-powered characters called "Shapes." Think of Shapes as intelligent, conversational AI companions that can help you with everything from learning and creativity to entertainment and productivity.

## Core Concept

Each Shape is a unique AI character with:

- **Distinct Personality**: Their own communication style, tone, and quirks
- **Specialized Knowledge**: Expertise in specific areas or topics
- **Persistent Memory**: They remember your conversations and preferences
- **Custom Capabilities**: Special features like image generation, web search, or voice chat

## How Shapes Work

### The Technology

Shapes are powered by advanced AI language models, but what makes them special is:

1. **Custom Training**: Each Shape is fine-tuned for specific personalities and use cases
2. **Memory Systems**: They maintain context across conversations and remember your preferences
3. **Multimodal Capabilities**: Many can process and generate text, images, and voice
4. **Real-time Learning**: They adapt and improve based on interactions

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Are Shapes real people?" icon="question">
    No, Shapes are AI-powered characters. While they can have very realistic personalities and conversations, they are artificial intelligence, not human beings.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do Shapes really remember me?" icon="question">
    Yes! Shapes have memory systems that allow them to remember your conversations, preferences, and personal details you choose to share.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I create my own Shape?" icon="question">
    Absolutely! Anyone can create and customize their own Shapes. Check out our [Creator Guide](/creator-guide/create-shape) to get started.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Are my conversations private?" icon="question">
    Your conversations are private by default. You control what information Shapes remember and can adjust privacy settings for each character.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do I need to pay to use Shapes?" icon="question">
    Basic chatting with most Shapes is free. Premium features like image generation, web search, and advanced AI engines require a subscription.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
