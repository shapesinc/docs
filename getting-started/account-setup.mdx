# Account Setup and Homepage

Getting started with Shapes Inc is quick and easy! You can create your account using your existing Gmail, email address, or X account.

## Creating Your Account

You have three convenient ways to join Shapes Inc:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Gmail Account" icon="google">
    Sign up instantly using your Google account
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email Address" icon="envelope">
    Create an account with any email address
  </Card>
  <Card title="X Account" icon="x-twitter">
    Use your X account to sign up
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Shapes Inc">
    Go to [shapes.inc](https://shapes.inc) and click the Log in button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Your Sign Up Method">
    Select Continue with Google, Continue with Email, or Continue with X
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Registration">
    Follow the prompts to verify your account and set up your profile
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Exploring">
    You'll be redirected to the homepage where your journey begins
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Already have an account? Simply click Sign In and use the same method you used to create your account.
</Note>
![Shapes Inc Login](/images/login_faq.png)

## Navigating the Homepage

Once you've signed up, you'll land on the Shapes Inc homepage. This is your central hub for discovering, creating, and managing Shapes.

The search bar at the top is your gateway to finding the perfect Shape. It has four powerful search modes:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="AI Search">
    Describe what you're looking for in natural language. Example: "I need a helpful coding assistant" or "Find me a creative writing partner". Best for when you know what you want but not sure which specific Shape to choose.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Name Search">
    Search for Shapes by their exact name. Example: "Einstein" or "Creative Writer Bot". Best for when you know the specific Shape you're looking for.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Tag Search">
    Search using single-word traits or characteristics. Example: "helpful", "funny", "coding", "creative". Best for discovering Shapes with specific personality traits or skills.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Vibe Search">
    Search by feeling or mood. Example: "chill", "energetic", "mysterious". Best for finding Shapes that match a particular energy or atmosphere.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Below the search bar, you'll find the Create Shape button that lets you create your own AI character from scratch. Below that are popular tags you can click for instant searches like coding, creative, helpful, funny, gaming, and educational.

In the upper right corner, you'll find your profile section:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Profile Picture Menu">
    Click your profile picture to log out, generate an API key, or manage your account settings.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Premium Button">
    Located next to your profile picture to manage subscriptions, upgrade to premium features, or access the billing portal.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

The main content area shows different collections of Shapes:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Your Shapes" icon="user">
    Shapes you've created and own
  </Card>
  <Card title="Recent Interactions" icon="clock">
    Shapes you've chatted with recently
  </Card>
  <Card title="Popular Shapes" icon="star">
    Trending and highly-rated Shapes from the community
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

When you find a Shape you like, you can click to chat and start a conversation immediately, view details to learn more about the Shape's personality and capabilities, update or edit Shapes you own, or share and recommend Shapes to friends.

<Tip>
Start by chatting with a few popular Shapes to get comfortable with how they work before creating your own. Use the tag system to discover Shapes you might not have found otherwise, and connect with other users in our [Slack community](https://shapes.inc/slack).
</Tip>
