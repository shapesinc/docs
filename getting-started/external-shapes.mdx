title: "External Shapes"
description: "Learn how to interact with and personalize shapes created by other users"
---

If you're looking at the dashboard of a shape you don't own, the profile section stays the same, but the sidebar is different.

Now you can only see four tabs:

![Externalsidebar Pn](/images/externalsidebar.png)

## **AI Engine**

On this tab, you can:

- Enable engine overrides
- Adjust engine temperature for the shape

## Persona

On this tab, you can define your **User Persona**:

- Add a name for yourself
- Write a backstory about your shape

## Memory

View, delete, and write memories of your shape with you.

## Mail

This is your shape's email address. Clicking on it will:

- Copy the email address
- Open your default email app (if available)
