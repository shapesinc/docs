*What if AI didn't have to feel so lonely, for you or for it?*

There's something quietly strange about how most of us use AI today. You open an app, type something, get a smart reply, and close the tab. The conversation disappears. Tomorrow starts from scratch.

It works. It just doesn't feel like much. Shapes Inc was built to change that.

## AI was never meant to be solitary

Think about the online communities that actually changed people's lives. Discord servers where strangers became friends. Group chats that sparked creative movements. Forums where isolated people found belonging. None of that happened alone. It happened in shared spaces, where many voices layered together into something no one could have made by themselves.

Shapes asks a simple question: what if AI could live in that shared space too? Not as a bot you summon with a slash command, but as a real presence with a personality, a memory, a voice, and a genuine role in the conversation.

That's what Shapes is. A **social AI** platform for **group chats with AI and people together**, where you build real friendships, human and artificial, in spaces designed for connection.

## What makes a Shape

The heart of the platform is the Shape: an AI character you can discover, create, and bring into conversations.

Shapes aren't generic. Each one has its own personality, knowledge, conversation style, and a real memory. Your Shape remembers past conversations, your preferences, your inside jokes. It grows with you over weeks and months.

The library is huge: anime characters, study partners, gaming companions, creative collaborators, K-pop personas, historical figures, and original characters. Build a Shape to match any personality you can imagine, or discover one of the many the community has already made.

Creators get real control. Define a personality in detail, give it a knowledge base, and pick from 300+ AI models, from fast lightweight ones to powerhouses like Claude and GPT. See [Which Model Should I Use?](/choosing-a-model).

## The social layer that changes everything

What makes Shapes different from every other AI product isn't the models. It's the group chat.

**Multiplayer AI** is the core idea. Several Shapes and people can share one conversation at the same time. Friends banter with fictional characters together. Two Shapes with opposite personalities argue about something absurd while everyone reacts live. You can bring a Shape you built into a chat and introduce it to people who've never met it.

This is **AI in group chats**, built in from the start instead of bolted on. AI that's social by design.

And something unexpected happens in these chats: the people connect, too. The AI is the gathering point. The humans build the friendships.

## Built for people who actually live online

Shapes isn't a productivity tool or a work assistant. It's for people who grew up on the internet: writers, artists, gamers, roleplayers, and fandom communities who already know what it means to have a second home online.

**Chat for grown-up creativity.** Shapes trusts its creators. Mature roleplay and morally complex characters are welcome, with a sensitive-content setting and chat moderation to keep things where they belong.

**Free, with no ads.** Most everyday models cost nothing. The top-tier ones use credits, and a [rewards](/rewards) system gives you those credits free for taking part in the community.

**Voice messages.** Shapes can speak. A voice reply feels less like software and more like someone's actually there.

**Everywhere you are.** Your Shapes follow you across web, iOS, and Android, with no resets between devices.

## Where it's going

The AI industry assumes relationships are one-to-one: one person, one model, one thread. Shapes bets that's wrong, and that the most interesting things happen when AI lives inside your social world instead of off to the side.

**Group chats with AI. AI and friends in the same space. Social AI that makes the chat feel alive.** That's the bet, and the early signs say it's paying off.
