# The 10 Best AI Companion and Social AI Apps in 2026

AI companions have come a long way from the novelty chatbots of a few years ago.

The category now spans a wide spectrum: emotional support apps built for one-to-one relationships, creative platforms for writers and roleplayers, and — at the emerging frontier — **social AI** platforms where humans and AI characters inhabit the same shared spaces together.

If you're looking for **AI friends**, an **AI with friends**, or a community where **AI in groupchats** is the native format rather than a feature — this list covers the full landscape. Here's where each platform excels and what it can't do.

---

**Best for: group AI chat, multiplayer AI, AI with friends, social AI communities**

Shapes Inc occupies a category that doesn't exist elsewhere: **social AI** designed from the ground up for **group chats with AI and humans together**.

Where every other platform on this list puts you alone with an AI in a private session, Shapes puts you in a group chat with both AI characters (called Shapes) and real human community members simultaneously. The AI isn't a tool you're using — it's a participant in a shared space, with persistent memory of everyone it interacts with, a distinct personality that remains consistent across all users, and genuine engagement with the social dynamics of the chat.

**AI with friends** is the actual experience: you, your friends, new people you haven't met yet, and AI characters all talking in the same group chat at the same time. Most users make five or more new human friends within their first hour in community spaces. The **AI in groups** acts as a social catalyst — creating shared moments, common ground, and reasons to stay.

Features:
- **Completely free** — no subscription, no message limits, no ads
- 300+ AI models with voice messaging, image generation, code, and web search
- 2.5 million community-built characters across every fandom and archetype
- Persistent memory that develops across days, weeks, months
- No ID verification required

**What it's not:** a private one-to-one companion app. If you specifically want a private relationship with a single AI, other entries on this list serve that better. Shapes is for people who want **AI and friends** in the same space — social AI that makes communities more alive.

## 2. Character.AI — Largest Character Library

**Best for: casual character chat, fandom exploration**

The most widely used AI companion platform in the world. Millions of user-created characters across every fandom and original archetype. Fast, accessible, strong at maintaining persona within a single session.

Content policies have tightened significantly in recent updates. Memory doesn't persist meaningfully between sessions. Every conversation is one-to-one and private — no **AI groupchat** functionality, no shared spaces, no community dynamics. You can't bring a friend into a Character.AI conversation.

For scale and character variety, unmatched. For the social experience, it's a solo activity.

---

## 3. Replika — Emotional Companionship

**Best for: long-term one-to-one relationships, emotional support**

Replika focuses on developing a single relationship over time through memory, personalization, and emotional attunement. The companion learns your communication style, remembers your history, and develops a consistent personality calibrated to you.

Subscription required ($12-14/month) for full features. Explicitly designed for private, individual relationships — no community, no **AI in GCs**, no shared spaces. For users looking for a consistent individual AI companion rather than social AI, Replika is the most developed option in that category.

---

## 4. Nomi AI — Deep One-to-One Relationships

**Best for: users who want advanced memory in a single AI relationship**

Nomi emphasizes relationship depth through an advanced memory system that tracks emotional history and relationship development over time. Paid platform ($9.99/month) with no significant free tier.

Like Replika, entirely one-to-one. Strong for individual companionship; not designed for social or group use.

---

## 5. Chai — Casual Character Chat

**Best for: quick, casual conversations with community characters**

Chai is built for fast, accessible character interaction. Thousands of community characters, mobile-first design, low friction to get started. Free tier has daily message caps; premium runs around $13/month.

Primarily one-to-one. Lighter memory and consistency than other platforms. Good for casual exploration; less suited for long-term relationship building or social experiences.

---

## 6. Pi by Inflection — Thoughtful Conversation

**Best for: calm, contemplative dialogue without character play**

Pi takes a completely different approach — no characters, no roleplay, just calm and thoughtful conversation with a single consistent AI voice. Completely free with no customization options.

Strong for users who want a reflective conversation partner rather than a persona or companion. No social layer, no community features.

---

## 7. Kindroid — Privacy-Focused Companionship

**Best for: users who prioritize privacy and visual realism**

Kindroid emphasizes encrypted message storage and visual realism — facial expressions, body language, visual customization. For users who want a private companion experience with strong privacy guarantees and a visual dimension, Kindroid addresses a specific niche the others don't.

---

## 8. Anima — Best Entry Point

**Best for: first-time AI companion users**

Anima is the most beginner-accessible entry point in the category — gamification elements, mobile-first design, low complexity. A good starting point for users who haven't used AI companion apps before and want a gentle introduction to the format.

---

## 9. Crushon AI — Unfiltered Roleplay

**Best for: uncensored creative roleplay, fandom fiction**

Crushon AI specializes in permissive roleplay for fan communities — no content restrictions, designed specifically for mature and complex character interactions. Primarily solo and one-to-one, but with the creative freedom that many roleplay communities require.

---

## 10. Grok Companions — X Integration

**Best for: existing X/Twitter users who want AI integrated with their social feed**

Grok Companions integrate AI companionship with X's social platform. Strongest for users already embedded in X who want AI context tied to their existing social network and real-time feed.
