1. **Visit the Shapes Website**: Go to [shapes.inc](https://shapes.inc) and log in or create a new account.
1. Click **Search** in the sidebar, or click **New Chat** if you want to add a Shape to a new chat.
2. **Search for a Shape**: There are several search options.
   - **Name Search** - Search by a character specific name or shape specific name if you know it, i.e. "Woody", "Sonic", "Cloud"
   - **Tag Search** - Search by tags, can be specific or categories. i.e. "genshin impact", "mental health", "art", "k-pop"
   - **Vibe Search** - Known as our semantic search where we search for the meaning behind your words rather than just matching keywords. i.e. "winter", "mythology", "kingdom", "ancient"
3. **Select your shape**: Scroll through the results and click on **"Chat Now"** to begin chatting

In order for a Shape to show up in search, the creator of the Shape must have the search option toggled on. Additionally, the Shape must not be marked as sensitive.
