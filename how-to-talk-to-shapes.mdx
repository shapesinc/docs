title: "How to find Shapes"
description: "Search for Shapes!"
---


1. **Visit the Shapes Website**: Go to [shapes.inc](http://shapes.inc) and log in or create a new account.
2. **Search for a shape**: There are 4 ways you can currently search our website to get different types of results\!
   - **Name Search** - Search by a character specific name or shape specific name if you know it, i.e. "Woody", "Sonic", "Cloud" 

     ![Namesearch Avi](/images/namesearch.avif)
   - **AI Search** - Like an intelligent research assistant, tell it what you want and it will bring back various result options that match. i.e. "for my self care server", "a shape to play horror-themed games and tell scary stories", "roblox script expert" 

     ![A Isearch Avi](/images/AIsearch.avif)
   - **Tag Search** - Search by tags, can be specific or categories. i.e. "genshin impact", "mental health", "art", "k-pop" 

     ![Tagsearch Avi](/images/tagsearch.avif)
   - **Vibe Search** - Known as our semantic search where we search for the meaning behind your words rather than just matching keywords. i.e. "winter", "mythology", "kingdom", "ancient" 

     ![Vibesearch Avi](/images/vibesearch.avif)
3. **Select your shape**: Scroll through the results and click on **"Chat Now"** to begin chatting

![Searchresultssonic Avi](/images/searchresultssonic.avif)

In order for a Shape to show up in search, the creator of the Shape must have the search option toggled on. Additionally, the Shape must not be marked as sensitive. 

**Need more help? Head over to **[**Our Reddit Page**](https://www.reddit.com/r/ShapesInc/)** where you can get additional support in our community.**
