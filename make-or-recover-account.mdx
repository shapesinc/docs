<Tabs>
  <Tab title="New User">
    How to Make a Shapes Account for New Users

    **Step 1**\
    Head over to Shapes.inc. Select the Sign in button found underneath the search bar. \
    \
    **Step 2**\
    Select either Email or Google as your option. \
    \
    **Step 3**\
    Either go through Google Sign in, or enter in your Email and create a password. Email users will need to verify their emails before being able to access their account.

    ![Signin Pn](/images/signin.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Existing User">
    Recover your Shapes account: directions for existing users

    **Step 1**\
    Head over to our website, Shapes.inc. Select the Sign in button found underneath the search bar. \
    \
    **Step 2**\
    Select the method you used to sign into your account previously - Either Google or Email. For email, you'll need to head to the bottom where it says, "Already have an account? Sign in."  \
    \
    **Step 3**\
    If you can't sign in via Google, you will need to recover your account with Google. If you forgot your password for Email sign in, please utilize the forgot password button on the Log in page.

    <Tip>
      Still having trouble signing in? Send us an email: [hi@shapes.inc](mailto:hi@shapes.inc)
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
