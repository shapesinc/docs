<Tabs>
  <Tab title="New User">
    How to Make a Shapes Account for New Users

    **Step 1**\
    Head over to Shapes.inc. Select the Log in button found in the top right corner. \
    \
    **Step 2**\
    Select either Email or Google as your option. \
    \
    **Step 3**\
For Google, you can select Create an account on the next step. For Email, you can find the Sign up button below the Continue section. 

        ## Quick Links
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Existing User">
    Recover your Shapes account: directions for existing users
    \
    **Step 1**\
    Head over to our website, Shapes.inc. Select the Log in button found in the top right corner. \
    \
    **Step 2**\
    Select the method you used to sign into your account previously - Either Google or Email.\
    \
    **Step 3**\
    If you can't sign in via Google, you will need to recover your account with Google. If you forgot your password for Email sign in, please utilize the forgot password button on the Log in page.\
    
<Tip>
  Still having trouble signing in? Send us an email: hi@shapes.inc
</Tip>
