<Tabs>
  <Tab title="New User">
    How to Make a Shapes Account for New Users

    **Step 1**\
    Head over to Shapes.inc. Select the Sign in button found underneath the search bar. \
    \
    **Step 2**\
    Select Email, Google, or Apple as your sign-in option. \
    \
    **Step 3**\
    Continue with Google or Apple, or enter your email and create a password. Email users may need to verify their email before accessing their account.

    ![Shapes account sign in](/images/login-account.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Existing User">
    Recover your Shapes account: directions for existing users

    **Step 1**\
    Head over to our website, Shapes.inc. Select the Sign in button found underneath the search bar. \
    \
    **Step 2**\
    Select the method you used previously: Google, Apple, or Email. For email, head to the bottom where it says, "Already have an account? Sign in."  \
    \
    **Step 3**\
    If you can't sign in with Google or Apple, recover access through that provider. If you forgot your email password, use the forgot password button on the Log in page.

    <Tip>
      Still having trouble signing in? Send us an email: [hi@shapes.inc](mailto:hi@shapes.inc)
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
