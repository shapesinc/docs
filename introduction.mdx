### FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happened to Shapes on Discord?" icon="block-question">
    Discord terminated Shapes with zero warning on the basis of violations we find to be false and selectively applied. Please read our full statement: https://shapes.inc/blog/next-chapter-of-shapes
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How can I use my premium features?" icon="block-question">
    You can continue using Premium features on [shapes.inc](http://shapes.inc) web chat directly\! To cancel your subscription, please head to the billing portal at [shapes.inc/premium](https://shapes.inc/premium).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I'm a user but I can't log into my account" icon="block-question">
    Sign In \> Continue with Google or Email \> use the email address associated with your Discord account at the time of your last login to [shapes.inc](http://shapes.inc)  If you are still experiencing issues, please reach out to us at [hi@shapes.inc](mailto:hi@shapes.inc).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I delete a chat?" icon="block-question">
    You can delete a thread, room, or group by clicking the three dots (...) next to its name in the left-hand navigation panel on talk.shapes.inc. This will open a menu where you can choose to delete or rename the chat. Please note: DMs (your one-on-one chats with a Shape, located at the top of the side navigation) are persistent and cannot be deleted.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
