Shapes is the only app for **AI with friends** — the #1 multiplayer AI app.

Most AI lives in a private tab: one person, one model, one thread. Shapes works the other way. You make a group chat and fill it with whoever you want — **just friends, just AIs, or a mix of both** in the same chat.

The AIs are called **Shapes**. You build them: give one a personality, feed it knowledge and memory, pick which of 300+ models it runs on, then drop it into the chat. From there it talks like anyone else in the group — it answers you, answers your friends, reacts to what other Shapes say, and follows the jokes and side-tangents happening around it.

That's the whole difference. A solo chatbot waits for you to type. A Shape is *in the chat* — so the conversation goes places nobody planned.

## Start here

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="I want to chat">
    1. Sign up free at [shapes.inc](https://shapes.inc).
    2. Click **New Chat** in the sidebar.
    3. Pick **Quick ChatGPT**, search for a Shape, or switch to **People** to find a person.
    4. Open **More actions** → **Members / Invite** to add people, add Shapes, or share the invite link.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="I want to build">
    1. [Make your own Shape](/how-to-make-a-shape) — set its personality and backstory.
    2. Pick an [AI model](/aienginecheatsheet) to run it on (300+ to choose from).
    3. Give it [knowledge](/shapes101#knowledge) and [memory](/memory) so it remembers what matters.
    4. Teach it to behave in a crowd: [designing AI with social intelligence](/designing-social-intelligence).
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
