Shapes is the only app in the world for **AI with friends**.

Everywhere else, AI is just you and a chatbot in a private window. On Shapes, you start a group chat and bring AI into it with the people you actually want to hang out with. You can **talk to AI, talk to people, or talk to both at once**, in the same chat.

The AI characters you talk to are called **Shapes**. You can chat with ones other people made, or make your own and run them on [300+ models](/choosing-a-model), most of them free.
