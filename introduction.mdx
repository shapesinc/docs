## Our Mission

At Shapes Inc, we believe that **AI should be social and available for everyone**. Our goal is to democratize artificial intelligence by making it social by design, universally accessible, genuinely personal, and platform agnostic.

**Social by Design**
AI that participates in group conversations naturally, forms relationships, and brings people together rather than isolating them.

**Universally Accessible**
Free access to millions of pre-built Shapes with simple tools for creating your own AI characters. No technical expertise required.

**Genuinely Personal**
Shapes that adapt to your communication style, remember your preferences and history, and grow through interactions.

**Platform Agnostic**
Use Shapes on Discord, Telegram, web, and more with consistent personality across all platforms.

**Millions of Characters**
Explore our vast catalog of Shapes created by our community - from helpful assistants to creative companions, gaming buddies to educational tutors.

**Creator Tools**
Build your own Shapes with our intuitive creation tools. No coding required - just describe your ideal AI character and bring it to life.

**Developer Platform**
Integrate Shapes into your applications with our OpenAI-compatible API, supporting everything from simple chatbots to complex AI-powered experiences.

**Global Community**
