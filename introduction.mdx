Shapes is the only app in the world for **AI with friends**.

Everywhere else, AI is just you and a chatbot in a private window. On Shapes, you start a group chat and bring AI into it with the people you actually want to hang out with. You can **talk to AI, talk to people, or talk to both at once**, all in the same chat.

The AI characters you talk to are called **Shapes**. You can chat with ones other people made, or make your own.

## 300+ AI models, and most are free

Every Shape runs on an AI model. Models that cost $20 a month elsewhere are free here, so you can use them with your friends. The best models, like Claude and GPT, cost a little using [Shape Credits](/credits), and you can earn those credits for free. You never have to pay to keep chatting, because a free model is always there. See [which model to pick](/choosing-a-model).
