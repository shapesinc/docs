Shapes is the only app in the world for **AI with friends** — the #1 multiplayer AI app.

Everywhere else, AI is a private tab: one person, one model, one thread. On Shapes, you **talk to AI, people, or both** in the same chat. You can make your own AIs (we call them **Shapes**), give them a personality and unlimited knowledge, run them on 300+ models, and drop them into a group chat with the people you actually want to be around.

That one shift — AI inside a shared chat instead of alone in a separate window — changes everything. It's the difference between asking an assistant a question and being in a room where humans and AI riff off each other in real time.

## 300+ AI models — and most are free

Models that cost $20/month elsewhere are free to use on Shapes, so you can talk to them with friends. Browse the live list at [shapes.inc/engines](https://shapes.inc/engines).

A quick honest note on pricing: most engines are **free** and cost nothing to use. The frontier **premium** engines use [Shape Credits](/credits) — which you can buy or earn for free through [rewards](/rewards). You never have to pay to keep chatting; a free engine is always available. See the [AI Engine Guide](/aienginecheatsheet) to pick one.
