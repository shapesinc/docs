Shapes Inc is a platform that transforms how we interact with artificial intelligence. Instead of cold, impersonal chatbots, we've created **Shapes** - AI Social agents with rich personalities and memories.

## Our Mission

At Shapes Inc, we aim to democratize artificial intelligence by making it social by design, universally accessible, genuinely personal, and platform agnostic. Shapes engage naturally in group conversations, build relationships, and foster connection rather than isolation. Shapes are freely accessible, offering millions of pre-built Shapes and intuitive tools for creating custom AI social agents without needing technical skills. Each Shape adapts to your communication style, remembers preferences and history, and evolves through interaction. Shapes work seamlessly across platforms like WhatsApp, Telegram, and the web, maintaining a consistent personality everywhere.

Explore millions of user-created Shapes - from friends and assistants to gaming buddies and tutors. Use our no-code [Creator Tools](/creator-guide/create-shape) to build your own AI by simply describing it. Developers can integrate Shapes via our [**Shapes API**](https://github.com/shapesinc/shapes-api) for everything from basic chatbots to advanced AI experiences.
