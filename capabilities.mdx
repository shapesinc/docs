A Shape starts as a great conversationalist. With capabilities, it becomes a collaborator — it can see, speak, search, build, and remember. This page is the complete catalog: what every capability does and how to switch it on. For recipes that combine them into something delightful, see the [Skill Ideas cookbook](/shapeskills).

## Talk

The foundation. Shapes hold real conversations, not one-shot answers.

- **Natural replies** that respond to the whole room — you, your friends, and other Shapes.
- **Multiple messages** — a Shape can break a reply into a few messages, the way a person texts. See [Multiple Messages](/multimessages).
- **Regenerate, edit, reply, branch** — manage any message from its three-dot menu. See [Message Actions](/messageactionsmenu) and [Regeneration](/regeneration).

## Knowledge & the web

- **Web search & browsing** — a Shape can search the web and read pages with `/web` or on its own. Drop a link in chat and it can read and summarize it.
- **Read files & documents** — Shapes can read PDFs, Word and Office docs, slides, spreadsheets, text and code files, and more. Just attach the file.

### Custom tools (MCP)

Beyond the built-ins, you can connect your own tools through MCP providers like Smithery — image, video, and music generation, GitHub, web scraping, and far more. Full setup and a big list of ideas live in [Shape Skills](/shapeskills).

## Memory

Shapes remember, which is what makes a conversation feel continuous.

- **Short-term memory** keeps the current conversation coherent.
- **Long-term memory** lets a Shape recall people, decisions, and inside jokes across conversations.

Manage it live with `/sleep`, `/wack`, and `/reset` — see the [Memory Guide](/memory) and [Commands Reference](/commands).

## Little touches

The details that make a Shape feel alive:

- **Time awareness** — turn it on per chat and a Shape knows when each message was sent and how much time has passed, so "long time no see" actually means something. See [Designing Social Intelligence](/designing-social-intelligence).
- **Personas** — each person can set a [persona](/personas) so a Shape knows what to call them and what to remember, treating everyone in a room a little differently.
- **Voice-call games** — in a live [voice call](/voicecalls), the room can play Whiteboard or Zombie Tag together. See [Games](/games).

## Turn capabilities on

Capabilities live in two places, depending on whether they belong to the Shape or to the chat:

- **Per Shape — Creator Dashboard.** Voice, image generation, memory, and the model all live in a Shape's [dashboard settings](/shape-settings). These travel with the Shape into every chat.
- **Per chat — AI Configurations.** Open **Chat Settings → AI Configurations** and toggle on the **Shape Skills** you want (web search, run code, GIFs, and more). Skills are managed one at a time, so you decide exactly what a Shape can reach for in that room.
- **Custom tools — Custom Skills.** In the same AI Configurations panel, open **Custom Skills** and paste an endpoint URL from an MCP provider (like Smithery) to give your Shape a brand-new tool. Step-by-step recipes are in the [cookbook](/shapeskills).
