---
title: "How do I find group chats?"
description: "Find public chats from Home or through friends."
---

On **Home**, check **Join a public chat**. This section appears below **Your Shapes**.

<div style={{ position: "relative", maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%" }}>
  <img
    src="/images/shapes-faq-mobile-home-public-chats-final.png"
    alt="Join a public chat on the Home page"
    style={{ width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
  />
</div>

If you do not see a public chat you want to join, make friends and check their profiles. People often pin their group chats on their profile.

You can also make your own group chat and invite people to it.
