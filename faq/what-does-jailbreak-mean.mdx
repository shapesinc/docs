---
title: "What does jailbreak mean?"
description: "Learn what presets and jailbreaks are and how to use them at the chat, persona, or Shape level."
---

On Shapes, Inc., a **jailbreak**—also called a **preset**—is a set of custom instructions that tells a Shape how to behave, speak, or format its replies.

For example, a preset can ask a Shape to:

- Reply in a specific tone or language
- Keep responses short or use a particular format
- Follow a roleplay style
- Act as a tutor, writing partner, or other type of helper

A jailbreak does not unlock an AI engine or guarantee that it will ignore the engine provider's rules. Different engines may interpret the same instructions differently.

See [Presets](/presets) for writing tips, placeholders, and example presets.

## Where can I add instructions?

| Method | What it controls | Who can set it? |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Chat jailbreak** | How every Shape behaves in one chat | A chat owner |
| **Persona** | How Shapes understand and interact with you | Any user for their own persona |
| **Shape preset** | How one Shape responds wherever it is used | The Shape's owner |

When more than one method is active, the instructions are used together. Avoid giving them conflicting directions, because the Shape may follow them inconsistently.

## Add a jailbreak to a chat

A chat-level jailbreak affects the Shapes in that chat only.

You must be a **chat owner** to change it. If you do not own the chat, **AI Configurations** will not appear in the Chat Menu.

1. Open the chat.
2. Tap the **chat title** at the top.
3. Under **Chat Owner**, open **AI Configurations**.
4. Find **Jailbreak**.
5. Open **Preset Templates** and choose a built-in preset, or keep **Custom instructions** selected and write your own instructions in the text box.

The instructions apply to future Shape replies in that chat. Other chats are not affected.

## Create and use a persona

A persona tells Shapes about **you**. It is useful when you want Shapes to know your preferred name, backstory, interests, or how they should interact with you.

You do not need to own a chat to use your own persona there.

### Create a persona

1. Open the main menu.
2. Select **Personas**.
3. Tap **Create New Persona**.
4. Enter **My Name** and **My Backstory**.
5. Optionally add a **Persona Picture**.
6. Tap **Create**.

On the Personas page, select **Set as Default** if you want Shapes to use that persona by default in your chats.

### Choose a persona for one chat

1. Open the chat and tap the **chat title**.
2. Open **Personal AI Preferences**.
3. Open **Persona** and select one of your personas.

This selection is personal to you. It does not change another participant's persona or require chat-owner permission.

## Add a preset to a Shape

A Shape-level preset controls the response style of one Shape across its chats.

You must **own the Shape** to edit it.

1. From **Home**, go to **Your Shapes** and select the Shape.
2. Open **Personality** under **Shape Settings**.
3. On **Shape personality**, open **Advanced**.
4. Under **Response Style**, choose a built-in style or enter your own instructions in **Custom Response Style**.

Use a Shape-level preset for behavior that should remain part of the Shape everywhere. Use a chat jailbreak when the instructions should apply only inside one chat.

## How should I write a preset?

Be direct and specific about what you want. Include the desired tone, response length, formatting, and any behavior the Shape should avoid.

You can use these placeholders:

- `{shape}` refers to the Shape
- `{user}` refers to the person interacting with the Shape

For example:

```text
{shape} responds like a friendly study partner. {shape} explains one idea at a time, asks {user} a short follow-up question, and keeps each reply under three paragraphs.
```

Test the preset by sending a new message. If the result is inconsistent, simplify the instructions, remove conflicts, or try a different engine.
