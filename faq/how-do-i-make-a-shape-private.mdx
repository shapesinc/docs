---
title: "How do I make a Shape private?"
description: "Hide a Shape from search from Shape Settings."
---

From **Home**, go to **Your Shapes** and tap the Shape. On the Shape page, open the sidebar menu and tap **Settings** under **Shape Settings**.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-shape-sidebar-light.png"
  alt="Shape settings sidebar"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>

Scroll to **Danger Zone** and turn on **Make Shape Private**. This hides the Shape from search.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-shape-delete-light.png"
  alt="Make Shape Private in Danger Zone"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>
