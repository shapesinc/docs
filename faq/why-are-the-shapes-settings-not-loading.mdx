---
title: "Why are the Shape's settings not loading?"
description: "Refresh your sign-in session when Shape settings do not load."
---

If you cannot edit your Shape or load Shape settings, your sign-in session may need a refresh.

1. Sign out of [Shapes, Inc.](https://shapes.inc).
2. Open `https://talk.shapes.inc/api/auth/logout`.
3. Sign back in at [Shapes, Inc.](https://shapes.inc).

After that, open the Shape from **Home** > **Your Shapes** and try the settings page again.

<Tip>
  Need more help? Ask in our [support chat](https://talk.shapes.inc/support).
</Tip>
