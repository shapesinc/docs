---
title: "How do I use the app?"
description: "A short beginner intro to using Shapes, Inc."
---

Shapes, Inc. is a chat app where people and Shapes can talk in the same chat.

On **Home**, you can start talking to a Shape, open your own Shapes, or make a new one. Use **Chats** to find your conversations. Use **New** to start a new chat. Use **Settings** to change things like theme, language, font size, streaming, and Shape reach-outs.

If you are new, start with the [Introduction](/introduction). It explains the main parts of the app and what to do first.

  Need more help? Ask in our [support chat](https://talk.shapes.inc/support).
</Tip>
