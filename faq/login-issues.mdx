# How to Access Shapes

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Q1. How do I log in to Shapes Inc?">
    Head over to [https://shapes.inc](https://shapes.inc/) and smash that dazzling **Log in** button at the top-right corner like a tech-savvy ninja.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Q2. Which sign-in method should I choose?">
    If your Discord account is Gmail-connected, hit **Continue with Google**. If not, embrace the power of **Continue with Email**!
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Q3. Will previously created shapes be restored?">
    You bet! Log in with the same email tied to your Discord account, and presto, all your magnificent Shapes will reappear under "Your Shapes."
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Q4. What if I used a non-Gmail email with Discord?">
    Opt for **Continue with Email**, then register using that email. This clever move ensures your existing Shapes are retrieved and linked to your shiny new login.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
