---
title: "How do I unsubscribe from emails?"
description: "Change email preferences for your Shapes, Inc. account."
---

Open your [account page](https://shapes.inc/account), scroll to email preferences, and turn off the emails you do not want.

Make sure you save your changes before leaving the page.
