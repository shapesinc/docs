---
title: "How do I edit my Shape's profile?"
description: "Edit a Shape profile from Home, Your Shapes, and the Shape page sidebar."
---

From **Home**, go to **Your Shapes** and tap the Shape you want to edit. On the Shape page, open the sidebar menu and tap **Profile** under **Shape Settings**.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-home-shapes-light.png"
  alt="Your Shapes on Home"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-shape-sidebar-light.png"
  alt="Shape settings sidebar"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>

Use this page to edit the Shape's public profile, like its avatar, banner, name, description, and profile details. Tap **View Profile** when you want to check how it looks to other people.

  src="/images/shapes-faq-shape-profile-settings-light.png"
