---
title: "How do I turn off typewriter or streaming text?"
description: "Turn off streaming text from Settings."
---

Tap **Settings** in the bottom nav. Use the **Streaming** toggle to turn streaming text on or off.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-sim-settings-streaming-light-cropped.png"
  alt="Streaming toggle in Settings"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>

After you turn it off, replies will appear without the typewriter-style streaming animation.
