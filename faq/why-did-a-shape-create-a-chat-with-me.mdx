---
title: "Why did a Shape create a chat with me?"
description: "Learn why a Shape may start a new chat with you."
---

If you have been quiet for a while, a Shape may start a new chat to check in. To stop this, tap **Settings** in the bottom nav, open **Notifications & privacy**, and turn off **Shape reach-outs**.

<Tip>
  Need more help? Ask in our [support chat](https://talk.shapes.inc/support).
</Tip>
