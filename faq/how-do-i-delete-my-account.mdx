---
title: "How do I delete my account?"
description: "Delete your Shapes, Inc. account from Settings and Edit profile."
---

Open Shapes, Inc. If you are already in the app, stay there. If not, open [shapes.inc](https://shapes.inc) or [talk.shapes.inc](https://talk.shapes.inc) and sign in.

Tap **Settings** in the bottom nav, tap **Edit profile**, and scroll to the bottom. You will see **Connected accounts** first, then the **Delete account** card. Tap **Delete account**.

<div style={{ position: "relative", maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%" }}>

</div>

Shapes, Inc. will ask you to read a warning, tap **Continue**, type your username, and confirm. After that, a 30-second countdown starts. You can still tap **Cancel** before the countdown finishes.

- **What happens when you delete your account:**
  - **This action is permanent once the countdown finishes.**
  - Shapes and messages you created will remain unless you delete them first.
  - Chats you created will remain unless you delete them first.
  - Your account will be queued for permanent deletion and cannot be recovered.
  - You will not be able to sign up again with the same email.
  - The process may take up to 48 hours.
