---
title: "How do I edit my profile?"
description: "Edit your own Shapes, Inc. profile, bio, avatar, and banner."
---

In the app, tap **Settings** in the bottom nav, then tap **Edit profile**.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-sim-profile-settings-light-cropped.png"
  alt="Edit profile button in mobile Settings"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>

On web, open the sidebar menu and choose **Account**. That is the right web path for opening and editing your own profile.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-web-account-option.png"
  alt="Account option in the web sidebar menu"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>

On your profile page, tap the **pencil icon** next to the thing you want to edit. Use the pencil icons for your avatar, banner, name, and username. Use **Edit bio** to update your bio.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-sim-profile-page-light-cropped.png"
  alt="Profile edit controls"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>
