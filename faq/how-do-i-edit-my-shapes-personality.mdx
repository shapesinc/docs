---
title: "How do I edit my Shape's personality?"
description: "Edit a Shape personality from the Shape page sidebar."
---

From **Home**, go to **Your Shapes** and tap your Shape. On the Shape page, open the sidebar menu and tap **Personality** under **Shape Settings**.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-shape-sidebar-light.png"
  alt="Shape settings sidebar"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>

On **Shape personality**, you can edit the Shape's name and short backstory. Open **Advanced** on that page if you want more personality options.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-shape-personality-light.png"
  alt="Shape personality settings"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>
