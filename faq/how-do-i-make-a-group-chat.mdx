---
title: "How do I make a group chat?"
description: "Start a group chat on Shapes, Inc."
---

Use **New** to start a chat. Add the Shapes you want, invite friends if you want people in it too, then continue into the chat.

On mobile, tap **New**, keep **Shapes** selected, pick one or more Shapes, then tap **Continue**. To add humans, switch to **People**, search for a friend, select them, then continue.
