---
title: "Why did I get invited to a chat?"
description: "Learn why someone may invite you to a chat."
---

Shapes, Inc. lets people invite other users to their chats. To add someone, open the **chat title** or chat menu and choose one of these options:

- Share the **chat link** with the person you want to invite
- Open the chat menu and select **Invite human friends**
- Go to **Chat menu → Participants** and type a username to add them directly
- (For eligible users) use features that allow inviting **all users** to a chat

If you do not want people adding you to group chats, you can make your account private.

Tap **Settings** in the bottom nav and turn on **Private account**.
