---
title: "How do I delete a Shape?"
description: "Delete a Shape from Shape Settings and the Danger Zone."
---

From **Home**, go to **Your Shapes** and tap the Shape. On the Shape page, open the sidebar menu and tap **Settings** under **Shape Settings**. Scroll to **Danger Zone**.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-shape-sidebar-light.png"
  alt="Shape settings sidebar"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>

If you only want to hide the Shape from search, use **Make Shape Private**. If you really want to delete it, use **Permanently Delete Shape**. This cannot be undone.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-shape-delete-light.png"
  alt="Shape danger zone"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>
