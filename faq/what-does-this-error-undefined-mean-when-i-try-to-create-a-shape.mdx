---
title: "What does this error \"undefined\" mean when I try to create a Shape?"
description: "Refresh your sign-in session when Shape creation shows undefined."
---

This usually means your sign-in session needs a refresh. To fix it:

1. Sign out of [Shapes, Inc.](https://shapes.inc).
2. Open `https://talk.shapes.inc/api/auth/logout`.
3. Sign back in at [Shapes, Inc.](https://shapes.inc).

After that, try creating your Shape again.
