---
title: "How do I change the language?"
description: "Change the language used by the app."
---

Tap **Settings** in the bottom nav. Scroll to **App language**, tap the dropdown that shows your current language, then choose the language you want.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-sim-settings-language-light-cropped.png"
  alt="App language in Settings"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>

The app updates to the language you choose.
