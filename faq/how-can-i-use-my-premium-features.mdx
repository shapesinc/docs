---
title: "How can I use my premium features?"
description: "Use Premium features and manage payment history on Shapes, Inc."
---

Open Shapes, Inc. If you are already in the app, stay there. If not, open [shapes.inc](https://shapes.inc) or [talk.shapes.inc](https://talk.shapes.inc) and sign in.

You can use Premium features from the app after you sign in. To cancel a subscription or check payments, open [shapes.inc/premium](https://shapes.inc/premium) and go to **Payment History**.

<Tip>
  Need more help? Ask in our [support chat](https://talk.shapes.inc/support).
</Tip>
