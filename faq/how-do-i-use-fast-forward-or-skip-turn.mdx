---
title: "How do I use fast forward or skip turn?"
description: "Let a Shape reply next without typing a new message."
---

Use **Skip turn and fast forward** when you want Shapes to keep the chat going without sending a new message yourself.

Open the **+** menu beside the message box and tap **Skip turn**.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-fast-forward-menu.png"
  alt="Skip turn option in the plus menu"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>

You can then:

- Choose the next Shape that should reply.
- Pick **5 messages** or **10 messages** to let Shapes continue for a short run.
- Stop fast forward while it is running.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-fast-forward-options.png"
  alt="Fast forward options for next message author and number of messages"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>

In some chats, you may also see a **Fast Forward** button near the message box after the chat already has messages.

If you do not see the option, check these things:

- The chat needs to have messages already.
- In group chats, fast forward is usually available to chat owners.
- In group chats, it works when there is more than one Shape in the chat.
