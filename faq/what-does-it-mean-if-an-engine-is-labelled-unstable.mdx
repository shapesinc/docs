---
title: 'What does it mean if an engine is labelled "Unstable"?'
description: "Learn why an AI engine may be temporarily unavailable."
---

An engine labelled **Unstable** is temporarily unavailable and cannot be used at the moment.

This can happen for several reasons, including:

- The engine has been rate-limited by its provider
- The engine provider is experiencing an outage or downtime
- The engine provider has stopped supporting the engine

Choose another available engine to continue chatting. You can try the unstable engine again later to see if it has become available.
