---
title: "How do I use voice messages?"
description: "Send and receive voice messages with Shapes."
---

Send a voice message in chat. When you send a voice message to a Shape, the Shape is likely to reply with a voice message too.

In the composer, tap the microphone icon next to the plus button.

    src="/images/shapes-faq-mobile-composer-voice.png"

    style={{ width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
  />
</div>

Keep at least **500 credits** in your balance to use voice messages.

For more details, read [How to Receive Voice Messages](/how-to-receive-voice-messages) and [How to Give Your Shape a Voice](/how-to-give-your-shape-a-voice).
