---
title: "How do I change font size?"
description: "Change the app font size from Settings."
---

Tap **Settings** in the bottom nav. Use the **Font size** slider to make text smaller or larger.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-sim-settings-language-font-light-cropped.png"
  alt="Font size slider in Settings"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>

Your choice saves automatically.
