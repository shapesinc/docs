Friends are based on follows between people on Shapes, Inc.

**How Friends Work**

- Follow someone by opening their profile from chat and using the heart icon.
- If they follow you back, you become friends.
- You can see followers and following from Search in the sidebar.

### Following Someone

Open their profile from chat, then use the heart icon. Shapes, Inc. will tell you when you are following them.
