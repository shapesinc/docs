---
title: "How do I delete a chat?"
description: "Delete or hide a chat from the chat menu."
---

On web, open the sidebar and click the three dots next to the chat name. Choose **Delete** from the menu.

Some older direct chats cannot be deleted. For those, you can delete individual messages or hide the chat from your sidebar.
