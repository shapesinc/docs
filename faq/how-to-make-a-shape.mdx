---
title: "How to make a Shape?"
description: "Create a Shape from the mobile Home page in Shapes, Inc."
---

Open Shapes, Inc. If you are already in the app, stay there. If not, open [shapes.inc](https://shapes.inc) or [talk.shapes.inc](https://talk.shapes.inc) and sign in.

From **Home**, go to **Your Shapes** and tap the **New Shape** card.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-make-shape-home-light.png"
  alt="New Shape card on mobile"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>

On **Create a shape**, fill out **Basic**:

1. **Choose a name for your Shape**
2. **Short backstory describing your Shape**

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-make-shape-basic-filled-light.png"
  alt="Create a Shape Basic step on mobile"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>

You can open **Advanced** if you want more options. If the name or backstory is missing, Shapes, Inc. shows a warning and keeps you on this step.

When the required fields are filled, tap **Set up (1/2)**.

On **Complete your shape**, add the Shape images:

1. Add an **Icon / Profile Picture**. This is required.
2. Add a **Banner / Top of Profile** if you want one. This is optional.

<img
  src="/images/shapes-faq-make-shape-complete-light.png"
  alt="Complete your Shape image upload step on mobile"
  style={{ maxWidth: "390px", width: "100%", borderRadius: "1rem" }}
/>

Images can be up to 10MB each. After the required profile picture is added, tap **Create**.
