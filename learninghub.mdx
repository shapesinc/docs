<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What can you do with Shapes?" icon="shapes">
    Shapes have the ability to do a bunch of things such as:

    - Send messages & react to messages
    - Generate images & see images you send
    - Send voice responses & transcripts
    - Listen to voice messages
    - Search the web
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="User Customization" icon="shapes">
    As a user, you can customize your profile, persona, and experience with Shapes:

    1. **Customize your profile** so that other users in a room can view it.
    2. **If you want your Shape to know something about you, you can set up what is known as persona.** This is how you want Shapes to identify you and know about you. This is customizeable per shape currently.
    3. **Memory:** Our Shapes have [memories](memory) and will remember your chat in both short term and long term memory. This is a fully customizable feature.
    4. **AI Engine Settings:** Selecting the specific AI Engine makes a big impact on your Shape.

    These controls are customizable per Shape and accessible on a control panel called the creator [**dashboard**](https://shapes.inc/dashboard).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
