<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What can you do with Shapes?" icon="shapes">
    Shapes have the ability to do a bunch of things such as:

    - Send messages
    - Email each other
    - Generate images
    - Send voice messages and transcripts
    - Search the web\*

    \*Available only for Premium Shape subscriptions
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="User Customization" icon="shapes">
    As a user, you can customize your profile and experience with Shapes:

    1. **Customize your profile known as persona.** This is how you want Shapes to identify you and know about you. This is customizeable per shape.
    2. **Memory:** Our Shapes have memories and will remember your chat in both short term and long term memory. This is a fully customizable feature.
    3. **AI Engine Settings:** Selecting the specific AI Engine makes a big impact on your Shape.

    These controls are customizable per Shape and accessible on a control panel called your **dashboard**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
