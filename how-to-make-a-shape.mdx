![Log In](/1000002305.png)

1. **Sign in to** [**shapes.inc**](http://shapes.inc)

![Create A Shape](/1000002301.png)

2. **Click on "+ Create Shape"**
3. **Fill out the Basic settings for your Shape**
   - **Name:** Choose a unique name for your Shape
   - **Short backstory:** Write a brief description of your Shape's background
   - **Response style:** Select how your Shape communicates
   - **Custom response style field:** Add any specific style instructions

![Create A Shape 4 Pn](/Screenshot_20250820-133344.png)

## Advanced Settings Menu

4. **Customize Advanced settings (optional)**
   - **Initial message**: Set what your Shape says when conversations start
   - **Allow shape to send multiple messages**: Disable multi-message responses
   - **Personality traits**: Define key characteristics
   - **Tone**: Set the conversational tone
   - **Age**: Define your Shape's age
   - **Birthday**: Set your Shape's birthday
   - **Story**: Extended backstory with more detailed background information
   - **Likes**: What your Shape enjoys
   - **Dislikes**: What your Shape avoids
   - **Goals**: Your Shape's objectives in conversations
   - **Conversation examples**: Sample interactions to guide behavior
   - **Appearance**: Describe your Shape's visual characteristics

     ![Screenshot 20250820 133357 Pn](/images/Screenshot_20250820-133357.png)

## Custom Messages Menu

5. **Set up Custom Messages (Optional)**
   - **Wack message**: Confirmation message for resetting the conversation context. This doesn't delete long-term memories
   - **Error message**: What your Shape sends when it encounters an error
   - **Sleep message**: The message your Shape sends to confirm that it saved a long-term memory

     ![Screenshot 20250820 133445 Pn](/images/Screenshot_20250820-133445.png)

## Profile & Branding

6. **Complete your Shape's Profile**
   - **Profile picture**: Upload an avatar for your Shape
   - **Banner**: Add a banner image
   - **Category**: Choose the appropriate category
   - **Tagline**: Write a catchy one-liner
   - **SEO description**: Help others discover your Shape
   - **Tags**: Add relevant keywords
7. **Click "Create" to publish your Shape**
8. **Congratulations! Your Shape is ready to chat!**
