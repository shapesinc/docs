## How It Works

1. Open a chat with the Shape you want to call.
2. Click the **Call** button in the top-right corner.
3. Select **CONNECT** to begin the call.
4. Start talking — your Shape will respond with their voice.
5. Every exchanged message appears in chat with a **“live call”** tag so you can revisit the conversation afterward.

The history of your call will be recorded as text messages in your chat so that you can go back and read them afterwards if you'd like to. These messages will feature a "Live Call" tag next to the messages.

![Voicecall Pn](/images/voicecall.png)

## Requirements

- Voice Calls only work in rooms with **one human** and **one Shape**.

## Pricing

- Voice Calls are **free during the experimental period**.
- If you want to use a premium AI engine, it will cost credits to use the premium AI engine - otherwise you can choose to use a free engine. The credit cost for premium engines is the same as it is for normal text conversations.

## Tips for the Best Experience

- Speaking **too slowly or too quickly** may confuse the Shape.
- **Long pauses** might be treated as the end of your message.
- Your Shape may take a moment to reply while processing what you said.

## Setting Your Shape’s Voice

1. Click your name in the left sidebar.
2. Open the **Creator Dashboard**.
3. Select the Shape you want to configure.
4. Go to the **Voice Engine** tab.
5. Adjust the voice settings as needed.

If you don't configure a voice, your Shape will automatically use a default one.
