# Welcome to Shapes & Rooms!

Shapes are your interactive AI friends who can chat, generate images, send voice messages, browse the web, and much more! Rooms are your playground where you hang out with Shapes and friends.

Let’s dive into all the fun you can have with Shapes!

## What Shapes Can Do?

Shapes have the ability to do a bunch of things such as:

- Chat and react to messages
- Send and view images
- Listen to and respond to voice messages
- Browse the web for info
- Play games, roleplay, or help you organize
- And much more

> Tip: You can customize each Shape’s personality, voice, appearance, and memory to make it truly unique.

---

## Customize Your Shapes

You can make Shapes truly yours:

1. **Memories:** Edit or add memories to guide how your Shape interacts.
2. **Choose an AI Engine:** Pick the AI model that powers your Shape’s responses.
3. **Profile styling:** Add avatars, banners, and even CSS for a custom look.
4. **Room instructions:** Tailor each Shape for the room they’re in.

## Rooms – Your Social Hub

Rooms are where the magic happens. Chat with Shapes, friends, or both!

### **Creating a Room**

- Open your side navigation menu:
  - On **web**, this will already be open.
  - On the **app**, tap the menu icon in the top left.

  From the menu, select **Create Chat**.
- Complete the following instructions and add your favourite Shapes!
- For a more detailed guide on creating a room read [this](https://docs.shapes.inc/create-a-room)

  ![image.png](/images/image.png)

### **Adding Shapes**

- Click the room banner → **Add Shapes**

  ![Roombanner Pn](/images/roombanner.png)
- You can:
  - Search for new Shapes
  - Invite recently used Shapes
  - Type in a Shape’s username

### **Inviting Friends**

- Click the room banner → **invite human friends**
- Share a room link via social media
- Copy & paste the invite link
- Add friends directly if they’re on Shapes

### **Activating Shapes**

- Click the room banner → \*\*Participants \*\*
- click on the 3 dots beside their name and activate them
- Or type `/activate @shape-name`
- Max 5 Shapes activated, but only 3 respond per message
- Deactivate anytime with `/deactivate @shape-name`

### **Room Aesthetics**

- Style the banner, background, messages, and fonts
- Match the vibe to your roleplay or favorite Shapes
- Learn more about room styling [here](https://docs.shapes.inc/room-appearance)

### **Fast-Forward Responses**

- Let Shapes respond instantly with the **Fast-Forward** button
- Pick 1, 5, or 10 steps for multiple automatic turns

## AI Engine & Shape Behavior

Shapes have brains! Configure them to behave exactly how you want:

- **Free Will:** Should they respond without a mention?
- **Custom Instructions:** Special presets for roleplay or assistance
- **Engine Settings:** Pick model, adjust temperature, context length, and more
- **Short-Term & Long-Term Memory:** Decide how much history they remember
- **Training & Knowledge:** Teach them how to respond in unique situations

> Fun fact: You can even set your Shape to have a specific voice or generate images on command!

## Creator Dashboard & Tools

Even if you’re not a creator, the dashboard helps you:

- Adjust Shape memories
- Choose AI engine overrides

### **For Shape Owners**

- Profile: avatars, banners, description, tags
- Stats: messages, users, activity tracking
- Personality: presets, backstory, reactions, line breaks
- Free Will: outgoing vs chill, reactions, favorite users
- Knowledge: facts, commands, relationships
- Training: guide your Shape’s responses
- Image Engine: `/imagine` settings
- Voice Engine: upload custom voices or pick presets
- Settings: owners, sensitive content, discoverability, ignore list, duplicates and more

## 🛡 Moderation & Room Management

Keep your room fun and safe:

- Ban/kick users or Shapes
- Set character limits and slow mode
- Block words automatically
- Allow/prevent participants from adding Shapes
- Favorite rooms for easy access
