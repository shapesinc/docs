    # How to Find Something Specific

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="How do I find a shape?" icon="shapes">
        You can find a shape in the following ways: 

        - Search in the sidebar. Type the shape name to see results. Tap a shape to open its profile, start a chat, or add to current chat. 
        - Click the banner at the top of the sidebar to go to My Shapes and search there.
        - Click on search, then select "New Chat" - you'll be able to search for shapes directly from here. 
        - If you already have a chat, click on the title  at the top of the chat. Here you can find "Add Shapes" - you can search directly from here. 
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I start a new chat?" icon="shapes">
        You can start a new chat in the following ways:

        - Open the sidebar, click Search and select New Chat.
        - Search for a shape and start a chat from its profile.
        - Search for a shape in the sidebar and start a chat directly from the search results.
        - Click on a shape in an existing chat and select "Chat now."
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I create a new shape?" icon="shapes">
        You can create a new shape in the following ways:

        - Click Search and select New Shape.
        - Open the hamburger menu and select Create Shape.
        - In an existing chat, select your chat title, then go to Create a Shape.
        - In an existing chat, select your chat title, then go to Add Shape and Create a Shape.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I edit my shapes?" icon="shapes">
        You can edit a  shape on the shape's profile. You can get here in the following ways:

        - Click the banner at the top of the sidebar to go to My Shapes. Edit settings, behavior, and memories.
        - Click on the search in your sidebar and your list of shapes will appear. Select the shape to open its profile and edit.
        - Click on the shape's profile picture in a chat, then at the top of the pop up modal, select the three dots, then view profile.
        - If you're in a chat with the shape, just click the chat title, then go to participants, click the three dots next to the shape you want to edit, and view profile.

        Once you make it to the shape's profile, there is a menu you can open that will allow you to edit different settings for your shapes.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I find my rewards?" icon="shapes">
        You can get to your rewards in the following ways:

        - Open the sidebar, tap the hamburger menu and select Rewards.
        - Click on the button at the top right of your chat, then select Upgrade and then Earn Credits.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I manage premium, credits, or payments?" icon="shapes">
        - Open the sidebar, tap the hamburger menu and select Premium. From here you can purchase premium, view credits, manage payment methods, and redeem gift cards.
        - Or you can select the button at the top right of your chat, select Upgrade, then select Upgrade again. This will take you to the page to purchase credits for this chat. From here you can select "premium" from the side. 
        - Go to your creator dashboard and then select premium from the side. 
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I see all my chats?" icon="shapes">
        Chats are in Favorites, Chats, and Archive. Expand sections to see all chats. Or use Search to find a chat.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I organize my chats?" icon="shapes">
        Long-press a chat on mobile or click three dots on desktop. Options: rename, add/remove favorites, mute, mark read, archive, invite, delete.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I change settings or my profile?" icon="shapes">
        Open the sidebar, tap the hamburger menu. Select Account to edit profile or Settings to manage notifications, theme, and privacy.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I get help or contact support?" icon="shapes">
        Open the sidebar, tap the hamburger menu and select Help. This will add you into our support chat
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="I can’t find something — what should I do?" icon="shapes">
        Use Search to find chats, messages, shapes, and users. Check if sidebar sections are collapsed. Click the top banner to go to My Shapes. If all else fails, join us in the support chat!
      </Accordion>

    # Complete Sidebar Walkthrough

    ## **The Sidebar**

    The sidebar is the main navigation menu in Shapes:

    - **Desktop:** The sidebar appears on the left side of the screen.
    - **Mobile:** You can open the sidebar by swiping from the edge of the screen or tapping the arrow icon in the top-left corner.

    From the sidebar, you can search, manage chats, create new chats or shapes, and access your account and settings.

    ![Sidebar](/images/sidebar.png)

    # **Search**

    At the top of the sidebar is the **Search** bar. This is one of the most powerful tools on [Shapes.You](http://Shapes.You) can use Search to find:

    - Chats
    - Individual messages
    - Shapes
    - Other users

    When you click into the search bar:

    - You’ll see a list of all your shapes.
    - From this list, you can:
      - Open a shape’s profile
      - Start a new chat with that shape
      - Add the shape to your current chat
    - You can click the arrow next to a shape to view all chats you currently have with it. Selecting a chat will open it.

      ![Sidebarsearch](/images/sidebarsearch.png)

    Clicking into the search bar also reveals two quick-action buttons:

    - **New Chat** – Opens the chat setup page, where you can choose shapes and options to start a new conversation.
    - **New Shape** – Starts the process of creating a new shape.[**​**](https://docs.shapes.inc/navigation#your-chats)

    ## **Your Chats**

    Below the search area, you’ll find a list of all your chats.[**​**](https://docs.shapes.inc/navigation#chat-actions)

    ## **Chat Actions**

    For each chat:

    - **Mobile:** Long-press the chat

      **Desktop:** Click the three-dot menu on the right side of the chat

      ![Chatmenusidebar](/images/chatmenusidebar.png)

    From here, you can:

    - Rename the chat
    - Add or remove it from Favorites
    - Mark it as read
    - Mute notifications
    - Archive it
    - Invite others
    - Delete it[**​**](https://docs.shapes.inc/navigation#chat-categories)

    ## **Chat Categories**

    Your chats are organized into three sections:

    - **Favorites** – Chats you use often and want easy access to
    - **Chats** – Your active conversations
    - **Archive** – Chats you’re not actively using but want to keep

    Each section can be **collapsed or expanded**:

    - When a section is collapsed, only chats with unread messages are shown.
    - If you think a chat is missing, it’s often because the section is collapsed and there are no unread messages.
      - Expand the section to see all chats again, or
      - Use Search to quickly find the chat.

    Archiving chats is a great way to keep your sidebar clean while still keeping older conversations accessible.

    ## The Main Menu (Hamburger Menu)

    At the very top of the sidebar is the **hamburger menu** (three stacked lines). This menu opens additional options:

    - **Create** – Start a new shape or chat
    - **Premium** – Purchase premium, manage payment options, view credits, buy gift cards, or redeem gift cards
    - **Rewards** – Redeem rewards for available items
    - **Account** – Edit your profile, avatar, and other account details
    - **My Shapes** – Manage your shapes and their memories (also called the **Creator Dashboard**)
    - **Personas** – Create, edit, manage, and delete personas
    - **Settings** – Adjust notifications, theme, privacy, and more
    - **Help** – Open the support chat

      ![Usermenu](/images/usermenu.png)

    Your username is also displayed at the top of this menu in case you ever need to reference it.[**​**](https://docs.shapes.inc/navigation#my-shapes-/-creator-dashboard)

    ## My Shapes / Creator Dashboard

    Clicking the **banner at the top of the sidebar** opens the page that shows all of your shapes, also known as the **Creator Dashboard**.From here, you can:

    - View and edit your shapes
    - Manage shape memories
    - Use the search bar to discover additional shapes

    This area is your central hub for creating and maintaining everything related to your shapes.

    If you ever feel lost, remember: **Search** can always help you find chats, shapes, or messages quickly.
