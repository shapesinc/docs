    # How to Find Something Specific

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="How do I find a shape?" icon="shapes">
        You can find a shape in a few ways:

        - Click **New Chat**, stay on **Shapes**, and search by name or username.
        - Click **Search...** in the sidebar to search across chats, messages, Shapes, and people.
        - If you are already in a chat, click **More actions** → **Members / Invite** → **Add participants**, then stay on **Shapes**.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I start a new chat?" icon="shapes">
        You can start a new chat in the following ways:

        - Click **New Chat** in the sidebar.
        - Click **Quick ChatGPT** to start right away with a smart helper Shape.
        - Pick a Shape from **Shapes**.
        - Switch to **People** if you want to start with a person.
        - To make it a group chat, open **More actions** → **Members / Invite** after the chat opens.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I create a new shape?" icon="shapes">
        You can create a new shape in the following ways:

        - Click **New Shape** at the bottom of the sidebar.
        - In a chat, click **More actions** → **Members / Invite** → **Create shape**.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I edit my shapes?" icon="shapes">
        You can edit a Shape from its profile.

        Try one of these paths:

        - Use **Search...** to find the Shape.
        - Open a chat with the Shape, then go to **More actions** → **Members / Invite** and open the Shape from the participant list.
        - Click a Shape's avatar or name in a message if it appears in the chat.

        Once you are on the Shape profile, open its menu to edit settings, behavior, and memories.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I find my rewards?" icon="shapes">
        You can get to your rewards in the following ways:

        - Open the sidebar, click the menu button, and select **Rewards**.
        - In a chat, click **More actions** and select **Rewards**.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I manage premium, credits, or payments?" icon="shapes">
        - Open the sidebar, click the menu button, and select **Premium**.
        - In a chat, click **More actions** and select **Upgrade**.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I see all my chats?" icon="shapes">
        Use the chat list in the sidebar. You can filter it with **All**, **Unread**, **User DMs**, **Private**, and **Social**. You can also use **Search...** to find a chat.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I organize my chats?" icon="shapes">
        Long-press a chat on mobile, or click **More** next to the chat on desktop. The options can include rename, mute, mark as read, invite, archive, or delete.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I change settings or my profile?" icon="shapes">
        Open the sidebar, click the menu button, then select **Account** or **Settings**.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I get help or contact support?" icon="shapes">
        Open the sidebar, click the menu button, and select **Help**. This opens support.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="I can’t find something — what should I do?" icon="shapes">
        Use **Search...** to find chats, messages, Shapes, and users. If you still need help, open the menu and select **Help**.
      </Accordion>

    # Complete Sidebar Walkthrough

    ## **The Sidebar**

    The sidebar is the main navigation menu in Shapes:

    - **Desktop:** The sidebar appears on the left side of the screen.
    - **Mobile:** You can open the sidebar by swiping from the edge of the screen or tapping the arrow icon in the top-left corner.

    From the sidebar, you can search, manage chats, start new chats, create Shapes, and open your account menu.

    ![Sidebar](/images/sidebar.png)

    # **Search**

    At the top of the sidebar is **Search...**. Use it to find:

    - Chats
    - Individual messages
    - Shapes
    - Other users

    Use **New Chat** when you want to start a new chat. Use **Search...** when you want to find something that already exists.

      ![Sidebarsearch](/images/sidebarsearch.png)

    ## **New Chat**

    Click **New Chat** in the sidebar to start a chat.

    From there, you can:

    - Click **Quick ChatGPT** to start right away with a smart helper Shape.
    - Stay on **Shapes** and pick or search for a Shape.
    - Switch to **People** and search for a user.

    After the chat opens, use **More actions** → **Members / Invite** to invite friends, add people, or add more Shapes.

    ## **New Shape**

    Click **New Shape** at the bottom of the sidebar to make a Shape.

    ## **Your Chats**

    Below the search area, you’ll find a list of all your chats.

    ## **Chat Actions**

    For each chat:

    - **Mobile:** Long-press the chat

      **Desktop:** Click the three-dot menu on the right side of the chat

      ![Chatmenusidebar](/images/chatmenusidebar.png)

    From here, you can:

    - Rename the chat
    - Mark it as read
    - Mute notifications
    - Archive it
    - Invite others
    - Delete it

    ## **Chat Filters**

    Use the filter buttons above the chat list:

    - **All**
    - **Unread**
    - **User DMs**
    - **Private**
    - **Social**

    If you cannot find a chat, switch back to **All** or use **Search...**.

    ## The Main Menu (Hamburger Menu)

    At the top of the sidebar is the menu button. This opens account and app options:

    - **Premium** – Manage premium and credits
    - **Rewards** – Redeem rewards for available items
    - **Explore Fandoms** – Browse fandom lore pages
    - **Account** – Edit your profile and account
    - **Personas** – Switch how you appear in conversations
    - **Settings** – Adjust notifications, theme, privacy, and more
    - **Help** – Open the support chat
    - **Add account** – Add another account
    - **Log out** – Sign out on this device

      ![Usermenu](/images/usermenu.png)

    Your username is also displayed at the top of this menu in case you ever need to reference it.

    ## Chat Menu

    Inside a chat, click **More actions** in the top-right.

    Useful options include:

    - **Members / Invite** – invite friends, add people, add Shapes, or create a Shape
    - **Share chat** – share the chat
    - **Search** – search inside the chat
    - **Mentions** – see mentions
    - **Rewards** – open rewards
    - **Upgrade** – manage paid options
    - **Settings** – open the full chat menu

    In **Settings**, you can find pinned messages, media and files, participants, notifications, personal AI preferences, AI configurations, chat appearance, and moderation.

    If you ever feel lost, remember: **Search...** can help you find chats, Shapes, messages, and users.
