## How to Add Custom Room Instructions

1. **Open Your Room**: Go to the room you want to customize.
2. **Click the Room Title**: This will open the room menu.
3. **Select “AI Configurations.”**
4. Look for **“Custom Instructions for Shapes.”** This is your room preset box.

There is no character limit, so you can write as much or as little as you like.

## What Custom Room Instructions Can Do

### 🎭 Set the Scene

Turn your room into a cozy café, a spooky forest, or even a spaceship. This gives your Shapes the context they need to respond in the right mood.

### 🧑‍🏫 Give Your Shape Special Jobs

Assign roles like **helpful guide**, **creative writing partner**, or **game lore assistant**. This is perfect for focused conversations.

### 🧠 Save Important Memories

Tell your Shapes to always remember special moments, running jokes, or recurring storylines in this room.

### ✨ Create Unique Rooms

Each room can have its own personality and purpose. Keep your study room serious, your hangout room silly, and your roleplay room immersive.

## How It Works

Your room preset runs every time a Shape responds, just like a hidden instruction. This helps keep conversations consistent with the instructions you set.

> **Note:** Room presets do not override a Shape’s personality or make it easier to jailbreak. They simply add your extra instructions to every prompt so you do not have to type them out manually.

## Tips for Great Instructions

- Keep instructions clear and focused.
- Get creative. Each room can tell its own story.
- Experiment and have fun. Your Shapes will follow your lead!
- Change them up - the Shapes will know right away and adjust immediately.
- Keep in mind your [context window. ](contextwindows)
