## How to Add Custom Chat Instructions

1. **Open Your Chat**: Go to the chat you want to customize.
2. **Click the Chat Title**: This will open the chat menu.
3. **Select “AI Configurations.”**
4. Look for **“Custom Instructions for Shapes.”** This is your chat preset box.

There is no character limit, so you can write as much or as little as you like.

## What Custom Chat Instructions Can Do

### 🎭 Set the Scene

Turn your chat into a cozy café, a spooky forest, or even a spaceship. This gives your Shapes the context they need to respond in the right mood.

### 🧑‍🏫 Give Your Shape Special Jobs

Assign roles like **helpful guide**, **creative writing partner**, or **game lore assistant**. This is perfect for focused conversations.

### 🧠 Save Important Memories

Tell your Shapes to always remember special moments, running jokes, or recurring storylines in this chat.

### ✨ Create Unique Chats

Each chat can have its own personality and purpose. Keep your study chat serious, your hangout chat silly, and your roleplay chat immersive.

## How It Works

Your chat preset runs every time a Shape responds, just like a hidden instruction. This helps keep conversations consistent with the instructions you set.

> **Note:** chat presets do not override a Shape’s personality or make it easier to jailbreak. They simply add your extra instructions to every prompt so you do not have to type them out manually.

## Tips for Great Instructions

- Keep instructions clear and focused.
- Get creative. Each chat can tell its own story.
- Experiment and have fun. Your Shapes will follow your lead!
- Change them up - the Shapes will know right away and adjust immediately.
- Keep in mind your [context window. ](contextwindows)
