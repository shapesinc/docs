---
title: "Multiple Messages"
description: "Getting multiple messages from a Shape? Find out how to customize this setting."
---

There are two different settings that allow your Shape to send multiple messages in different circumstances.The first can be found on the **Shape Personality** page:

![Image Pn](/images/image.png)

This option lets your Shape occasionally send more than one message at a time. These additional messages use a different engine than your selected one.

\
The second setting, **Free Will**, allows your Shape to re-engage you if you haven’t responded. You can find this setting in your Room or DM settings by clicking the title or gear icon at the top of the page and navigating to **AI Configurations**.
