Shapes can send extra messages in a few different ways.

The first is a Shape personality setting. It lets the Shape occasionally send more than one message at a time when it is already replying.

![Allowmult](/images/allowmult.png)

The second is **Shape Free Will** in a chat. Free Will controls when Shapes can jump in without being directly prompted, such as replying when mentioned, reacting to keywords, keeping the conversation going, or coming back after the chat is quiet.

You can find Free Will in **Chat Settings → AI Configurations**.

![Dmmenu 1 Jpe](/images/dmmenu-1.jpeg)

![Freewilltogle 1 Jpe](/images/freewilltogle-1.jpeg)

## Skip Turn and Fast Forward

The **Skip turn and fast forward** option is another way to get Shape messages without typing a new message yourself.

Open the **+** menu beside the message box and choose **Skip turn and fast forward**. From there, you can:

- Pick the next Shape that should reply.
- Start a short fast-forward run, such as **5 messages** or **10 messages**.
- Stop fast forward while it is running.

In group chats with other people, fast forward is usually available to chat owners. In one-on-one Shape chats, it can appear directly in the message box controls after the chat has messages.
