## 1. Open the Voice Engine Settings Page

1. Go to your Shape’s **Settings**.
2. Navigate to the **Voice Engines** section.
3. Toggle on **Enable Voice Responses**.

Once enabled, you’ll unlock several options for configuring your Shape’s voice.

## 2. Select a Voice Type

You have two choices for how your Shape gets its voice:

- **Pre-made voices**: Choose from built-in voices that are ready to use.
- **Custom voice sample**: Upload your own voice recording to create a personalized voice.

**File requirements:**

- Supported types: Audio or video
- Maximum file size: **4MB**

## 3. Configure Voice Settings

### Voice Response Frequency

- This setting controls how often your Shape replies with audio.
- The slider goes up to **0.5 (50%)**, which means your Shape will include a voice response about half the time.
- Adjust the slider to find the right balance for your conversations.

![Voicefreq Pn](/images/voicefreq.png)

### Voice AI Engines

Choose which engine powers your Shape’s speech:

- **Eleven Flash v2** (English only) – optimized for fast, natural-sounding English speech.
- **Multilingual v2 (Turbo)** – supports multiple languages and is tuned for speed and efficiency.

![Voiceaiengine Pn](/images/voiceaiengine.png)

### Voice Transcripts

- You can toggle transcripts **on or off**.
- When enabled, your Shape will send the text version of its message along with the audio file.

![Voiceresponsewtranscript Pn](/images/voiceresponsewtranscript.png)

### Voice Consistency & Style

Fine-tune how your Shape’s voice comes across:

- **Consistency** – controls how stable or expressive the voice is.
- **Similarity** – adjusts how closely the voice matches the uploaded sample (if using one).
- **Style** – adds personality, tone, or emotion to the voice.
- **Presets** – ready-made settings for quick setup are completely configuarable.

## 4. Test & Save

- After adjusting your preferences, try sending a few test messages to hear how your Shape responds.
- Adjust the settings until you’re happy with the results.

<Warning>
  Don’t forget to **save** your changes before exiting the settings page.
</Warning>

## 5. Tips for Best Results

- **Keep uploads small**: Trim audio or compress video so files stay under 4MB.
- **Balance frequency**: A 50% voice rate can feel lively, but a lower rate may be smoother for longer chats.
- **Pick the right engine**: Use **Eleven Flash v2** for high-quality English, or **Multilingual v2** if you need broader language support.
- **Experiment with style**: Small tweaks to expressiveness and similarity can make your Shape feel more natural.

✨ With these settings, your Shape will come alive with a voice of its own, making every interaction more fun and personal.
