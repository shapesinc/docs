### FAQ

<Expandable title="What happened to Shapes on Discord?">
  Discord terminated Shapes with zero warning on the basis of violations we find to be false and selectively applied. Please read our full statement: [shapes.inc/explore?statement=true](https://shapes.inc/explore?statement=true)
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What happened to my Shape's memories?">
  Rest assured, your Shapes still have their memories on web and e-mail. We are working on finding a solution to make server memories available.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How can I use my premiium features?">
  You can continue using Premium features on [shapes.inc](http://shapes.inc) web chat directly\! To cancel your subscription, please head to the billing portal at [shapes.inc/premium](https://shapes.inc/premium).
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="I'm a user but I can't log into my account">
  Sign In \> Continue with Google or Email \> use the email address associated with your Discord account at the time of your last login to [shapes.inc](http://shapes.inc)  If you are still experiencing issues, please reach out to us at [hi@shapes.inc](mailto:hi@shapes.inc).
</Expandable>
